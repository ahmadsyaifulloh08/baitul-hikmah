# Baitul Hikmah — Visual Moodboard Per Era

> Setiap era memiliki identitas visual yang unik, mencerminkan semangat zamannya.
> Panduan ini digunakan untuk konsistensi ilustrasi, background, dan UI theming.

---

## 1. Pra-Islam (500–610 M)

**Primary**: `#8b949e` · **Tone**: Muted gray, sandy beige, dusty earth

### Mood & Atmosphere
- Dunia jahiliyah — kegelapan sebelum cahaya
- Padang pasir luas, langit malam berbintang, kesunyian spiritual
- Nuansa sepia, desaturated — seperti foto tua

### Visual Elements & Motifs
- **Geometric**: Simple repeating triangles & zigzags (motif tribal Arab)
- **Textures**: Sand grain, rough stone, woven textile patterns
- **Shapes**: Crescents (bulan sabit pra-Islam), bintang-bintang sederhana
- **Architecture**: Ka'bah silhouette, tenda Badui, pasar Ukaz

### Icon/Symbol Suggestions
- Bintang di padang pasir (navigasi karavan)
- Unta & karavan silhouette
- Ka'bah (bentuk kubus sederhana)
- Bulan sabit tipis

### Background Pattern
- Subtle sand dune waves (horizontal repeat)
- Faded tribal geometric (zigzag borders)
- Opacity: 4-6%, monochrome `#8b949e`

### Children Mode Adjustment
- Warmer tones (sandy gold instead of gray)
- Friendly camel characters
- Twinkling stars (Lottie animation)

## 2. Kenabian (610–632 M)

**Primary**: `#3fb950` · **Tone**: Emerald green, warm gold, radiant light

### Mood & Atmosphere
- Cahaya wahyu — harapan, transformasi, kebenaran
- Kehangatan Madinah, cahaya fajar, taman-taman hijau
- Luminous — seolah ada cahaya lembut dari dalam

### Visual Elements & Motifs
- **Geometric**: 8-pointed star (Rub el Hizb ✳) — simbol Islam
- **Textures**: Soft light gradients, gentle glow effects
- **Shapes**: Lingkaran cahaya, bintang 8 sudut, mihrab arch
- **Flora**: Daun kurma, zaitun, bunga-bunga Madinah
- **Architecture**: Masjid Nabawi sederhana (awal), Gua Hira silhouette

### Icon/Symbol Suggestions
- Bintang 8 sudut (Rub el Hizb)
- Cahaya/rays dari atas
- Mushaf (gulungan kitab)
- Mihrab arch
- Bulan sabit + bintang

### Background Pattern
- 8-pointed star tessellation (Islamic classic)
- Soft radial gradient (center glow)
- Opacity: 5-8%, `#3fb950`

### Children Mode Adjustment
- Extra bright green, golden sparkles
- Warm glow effects around text
- Animated light rays (subtle CSS animation)

## 3. Khulafaur Rashidin (632–661 M)

**Primary**: `#58a6ff` · **Tone**: Sky blue, clean white, silver

### Mood & Atmosphere
- Keadilan, ekspansi, keberanian — empat khalifah yang lurus
- Langit terbuka, cakrawala luas, peta yang mengembang
- Clean, noble, expansive — seperti udara pagi yang jernih

### Visual Elements & Motifs
- **Geometric**: Hexagonal patterns (sarang lebah — keteraturan & keadilan)
- **Textures**: Clean marble, parchment, woven mat (kesederhanaan)
- **Shapes**: Shield (perisai), scroll (piagam), sword (keadilan)
- **Architecture**: Masjid Al-Aqsa, Baitul Mal sederhana

### Icon/Symbol Suggestions
- Perisai dengan pedang
- Gulungan piagam/surat
- Peta ekspansi (garis-garis menyebar)
- Timbangan keadilan

### Background Pattern
- Hexagonal tessellation (clean, geometric)
- Expanding concentric circles (ekspansi)
- Opacity: 4-6%, `#58a6ff`

### Children Mode Adjustment
- Bright sky blue, fluffy cloud shapes
- Shield/knight imagery (ksatria keadilan)
- Map with friendly markers

## 4. Dinasti Umayyah (661–750 M)

**Primary**: `#d29922` · **Tone**: Gold, amber, warm bronze, white marble

### Mood & Atmosphere
- Kemegahan arsitektur, Damaskus yang megah, kekayaan & ekspansi
- Warm & grand — seperti matahari terbenam di atas masjid emas
- Monumental, architectural, sophisticated

### Visual Elements & Motifs
- **Geometric**: Interlocking squares & octagons (motif Umayyah klasik)
- **Textures**: Marble veins, gold leaf, mosaic tiles
- **Shapes**: Horseshoe arch (lengkung tapal kuda), dome, minaret
- **Architecture**: Dome of the Rock, Masjid Agung Damaskus, istana Umayyah
- **Decorative**: Vine scrolls (arabesques), palmette motifs

### Icon/Symbol Suggestions
- Dome of the Rock silhouette
- Koin emas (dinar Umayyah)
- Horseshoe arch
- Mosaic tile fragment

### Background Pattern
- Interlocking squares (Umayyah geometric)
- Vine scroll arabesque (horizontal border)
- Opacity: 5-8%, `#d29922`

### Children Mode Adjustment
- Bright gold, treasure-chest feel
- Castle/palace shapes (approachable)
- Shiny coin animations

## 5. Dinasti Abbasiyah (750–1258 M)

**Primary**: `#bc8cff` · **Tone**: Royal purple, deep gold, midnight blue

### Mood & Atmosphere
- Zaman keemasan Islam — Baitul Hikmah, ilmu pengetahuan, Baghdad
- Rich, intellectual, nocturnal — malam-malam para cendekia menulis
- Warm candlelight on parchment, ink & quill, astrolabe gleaming

### Visual Elements & Motifs
- **Geometric**: Complex 12-pointed star patterns (puncak seni geometri Islam)
- **Textures**: Aged parchment, ink splatter, brass/copper sheen
- **Shapes**: Astrolabe, armillary sphere, ink pot & quill
- **Flora**: Rose (Baghdad), lotus patterns
- **Architecture**: Baitul Hikmah, Baghdad gate, minarets of Samarra (spiral)

### Icon/Symbol Suggestions
- Astrolabe
- Buku terbuka / tumpukan kitab
- Spiral minaret (Samarra)
- Rumus matematika / angka Arab
- Inkwell & quill

### Background Pattern
- 12-pointed star complex tessellation
- Overlapping circles (scientific diagrams)
- Opacity: 5-8%, `#bc8cff`

### Children Mode Adjustment
- Bright purple + gold sparkles
- "Magic library" atmosphere
- Animated stars & planets (astronomy theme)
- Telescope/magnifying glass imagery

## 6. Fragmentasi (1000–1400 M)

**Primary**: `#f778ba` · **Tone**: Rose, dusty pink, steel gray, olive

### Mood & Atmosphere
- Dunia Islam terpecah tapi tetap hidup — Al-Andalus, Seljuk, Fatimiyah
- Mixed feelings — keindahan bersamaan dengan konflik
- Sunset colors — indah tapi menandakan akhir

### Visual Elements & Motifs
- **Geometric**: Andalusian zellige (complex multi-color tile patterns)
- **Textures**: Glazed ceramic, carved wood (mashrabiya), stained glass
- **Shapes**: Pointed/ogee arch (Al-Andalus), fortress towers
- **Architecture**: Alhambra, Masjid Cordoba, Madrasah Nizamiyah
- **Decorative**: Muqarnas (honeycomb vaulting), mashrabiya screens

### Icon/Symbol Suggestions
- Alhambra arch silhouette
- Zellige tile fragment
- Scholar's turban
- Ship (maritime trade)
- Fortress/castle tower

### Background Pattern
- Zellige-inspired tessellation (multi-geometric)
- Mashrabiya screen pattern (lattice)
- Opacity: 5-7%, `#f778ba`

### Children Mode Adjustment
- Soft pink + warm rose (less steel)
- Castle & adventure theme
- Scholar characters with big scrolls
- Colorful tile mosaics

## 7. Kemunduran (1200–1500 M)

**Primary**: `#da3633` · **Tone**: Deep red, charcoal, ash gray, faded gold

### Mood & Atmosphere
- Jatuhnya Baghdad, hilangnya Al-Andalus — refleksi & kehilangan
- Somber but dignified — bukan putus asa, tapi introspeksi
- Fading warmth — seperti bara api yang perlahan padam

### Visual Elements & Motifs
- **Geometric**: Broken/incomplete geometric patterns (fragmentasi visual)
- **Textures**: Cracked parchment, weathered stone, faded ink
- **Shapes**: Crumbling arch, scattered manuscripts, extinguished lamp
- **Architecture**: Ruins silhouette, burning library (Baghdad), Alhambra farewell
- **Symbolic**: Manuscripts being carried away (preservation of knowledge)

### Icon/Symbol Suggestions
- Broken arch
- Scattered/falling pages
- Dimming lamp/lantern
- Fortress in shadow
- Sunrise at horizon (hope — Islam continues)

### Background Pattern
- Fragmented geometric (intentionally broken repeat)
- Faded arabesque with decay effect
- Opacity: 3-5%, `#da3633` (subtler than other eras)

### Children Mode Adjustment
- Softer red → warm terracotta
- Focus on preservation theme (saving books!)
- Hopeful elements (sunrise, seeds growing)
- Less somber, more "lessons learned"

## Cross-Era Visual Guidelines

### Consistency Rules
1. **Era color** always present — as accent, border, or background tint
2. **Geometric patterns** increase in complexity as civilization advances (Pra-Islam → Abbasiyah peak → Kemunduran simplified)
3. **Architecture** evolves: simple tents → grand mosques → ruins
4. **Light direction**: Kenabian era = light from above; Kemunduran = dim/twilight

### Pattern Complexity Scale
```
Pra-Islam    ●○○○○  Simple triangles, zigzags
Kenabian     ●●○○○  8-pointed stars
Rashidin     ●●●○○  Hexagonal patterns
Umayyah      ●●●●○  Interlocking squares + arabesques
Abbasiyah    ●●●●●  Complex 12-point stars (peak)
Fragmentasi  ●●●●○  Zellige multi-pattern (diverse)
Kemunduran   ●●○○○  Broken/fading patterns
```

### Transition Between Eras
When scrolling timeline across era boundaries:
- Background color gradual blend (500ms CSS transition)
- Pattern crossfade (opacity swap)
- Timeline marker changes to diamond shape at era boundary
