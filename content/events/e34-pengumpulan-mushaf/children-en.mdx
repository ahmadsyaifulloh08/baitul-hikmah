# Collecting the Qur'an Into One Book 📖

## 🏛 Did You Know?

The Qur'an you read today wasn't always a **book**! In Prophet Muhammad's time, the verses were written on palm leaves, flat bones, and pieces of leather. And most importantly — hundreds of companions **memorized it by heart**! 🧠

## 🔬 What Happened?

After Prophet Muhammad ﷺ passed away, there was a war against people who turned away from Islam. In a big battle at **Yamamah**, about **70 people who had memorized the Qur'an** were killed! 😢

The companion Umar was very worried. He thought: *"What if more memorizers pass away? We need to collect the Qur'an into one complete written copy!"*

Umar convinced Caliph Abu Bakr, and they chose a smart young man named **Zayd ibn Thabit** — he was the person who used to write down the revelations for the Prophet!

Zayd said: *"By Allah, moving a mountain would be easier than this job!"* 😅

But Zayd was very careful. For every verse, he needed:
1. ✍️ **The original writing** — written in front of the Prophet
2. 👥 **Two witnesses** — who memorized that verse and saw it being written

So no verse could be included unless it was checked **twice**!

## 🌍 The Impact Reaches Us Today!

Thanks to the hard work of Zayd and the companions, the Qur'an you read today is **exactly the same** as what was revealed to Prophet Muhammad ﷺ over 1,400 years ago! 📖

Not a single letter has changed! Imagine — you are reading the exact same words that Prophet Muhammad ﷺ recited! How amazing is that? ✨

This proves Allah's promise:

> *"Indeed, it is We who sent down the Reminder, and indeed, We will be its guardian."*
> — Qur'an, Al-Hijr: 9

## 📖 Islam & Knowledge

Did you know that the way Zayd verified the Qur'an is similar to how **modern scientists** prove things? They also need **written evidence** and **witnesses/repetition** before something is accepted as fact!

So Muslims were practicing the "scientific method" 1,400 years ago! 🔬🌟
