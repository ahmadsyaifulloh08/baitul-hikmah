
# Saladin Frees Jerusalem ⚔️🕊️

## ⚔️ Why Did This Happen?

The city of Jerusalem (al-Quds) is very important to Muslims — that's where **Masjid al-Aqsa** is, the place where Prophet Muhammad ﷺ went on his Night Journey! 🌙

But for **88 years**, the city was controlled by Crusaders from Europe. They took the city by force, and many Muslim residents suffered.

Then came a hero named **Saladin** (Salah ad-Din al-Ayyubi)! 🦁

## 🛡️ How Did It Happen?

Saladin was a very smart and brave leader. First, he united the Muslims who had been divided. Then he made a brilliant plan!

At a place called **Hattin**, Saladin defeated the Crusader army in a huge battle. The enemy soldiers were very thirsty because there was no water, and Saladin had cut off their access to the lake. 💧

The Crusader King was captured! But Saladin **gave the king water** with his own hand. Even in war, Saladin showed noble character! 🤲

After that, Saladin went to Jerusalem. And when the city surrendered...

## 🕊️ What Happened After...

This is the most amazing part! When the Crusaders took Jerusalem 88 years earlier, they killed many people. But when **Saladin** freed Jerusalem:

- ❌ **No massacre** — not a single civilian was killed!
- 🏥 **Christians could stay** or leave safely
- ⛪ **Churches were protected** — none were damaged
- 💰 Saladin even **paid from his own money** to free people who couldn't afford the ransom!

Even Europeans who were his enemies wrote: *"Saladin is the noblest knight in the world!"*

## 💪 Values We Can Learn

1. **Win with dignity** — Saladin could have taken revenge, but he chose mercy. That's true strength!
2. **Justice for everyone** — He protected Christians and their places of worship, because Islam teaches justice for all people
3. **Unity is strength** — Saladin succeeded because he united Muslims who were previously divided

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said:

> *"Indeed, Allah is gentle and loves gentleness in all things."*
> — Sahih al-Bukhari & Muslim

Saladin won not through violence, but through **the nobility of his character**! 🌟
