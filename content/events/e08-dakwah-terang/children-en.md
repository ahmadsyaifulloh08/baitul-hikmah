# The Call from the Hill! 📢

## 🤫 From Secret to Open

Remember the story of the secret da'wah? For three years, Prophet Muhammad SAW invited people to Islam quietly. But now, Allah commanded something new:

"And warn your closest kindred! ﴾214﴿" (QS. Al-Shu'ara': 214)

It was time to speak openly! No more hiding. Prophet Muhammad SAW had to deliver the message of Islam to everyone — starting with his own family.

How nerve-wracking! Three years of secrecy, and now he had to stand before everyone and say: "I bring a message from Allah." But the Prophet was not afraid, because he knew this was Allah's command.

## 🍽️ The Family Dinner

The Prophet's first step was to invite his extended family — Banu Hashim — for a meal together. He prepared good food, and about forty relatives came.

After eating, Prophet Muhammad SAW stood and said: "O my family, I bring you something better than anything else — the good of this world and the Hereafter."

But before he could finish, his uncle **Abu Lahab** cut him off: "Enough! Everyone leave!" And the gathering broke up.

Did the Prophet give up? No! He held another dinner and delivered his message again. That's our Prophet — he never gave up!

## 📢 The Shout on Mount Safa

After inviting his family, Prophet Muhammad SAW did something incredibly brave. He climbed to the top of **Mount Safa** in the center of Mecca and shouted:

**"YAA SHABAAHAH!"** which means: **"DANGER AT DAWN!"**

This was like a fire alarm! Everyone in Mecca rushed over because that shout was used to warn of enemy attacks.

When everyone had gathered, the Prophet asked: "If I told you there was an army behind that hill about to attack you, would you believe me?"

Everyone answered: **"Of course! You have never lied to us!"**

Then the Prophet said: **"Then listen. I am a warner from Allah. Save yourselves from the Hellfire!"**

He called out to each family one by one. He even named his own daughter, Fatimah: "O Fatimah, I cannot help you before Allah. Save yourself!"

## 😤 Angry Abu Lahab

After the Prophet finished speaking, everyone was silent. Then Abu Lahab stood and shouted: **"May you perish! Is this what you gathered us for?!"**

Abu Lahab was furious. He was the Prophet's uncle who opposed Islam the most fiercely. His wife, **Umm Jamil**, even placed sharp thorns on paths the Prophet walked so his feet would be hurt!

But happened? Allah revealed a special surah about Abu Lahab — **Surah al-Lahab** — declaring that Abu Lahab's wealth and power would not save him.

The lesson: wealth and power mean nothing if our hearts reject the truth.

## 🌟 Lessons for Us

**Courage means speaking the truth.** Prophet Muhammad SAW knew people would be angry. But he delivered Allah's message anyway because it was the right thing to do.

**No favoritism in truth.** The Prophet even named his own daughter — meaning truth applies to everyone without exception, including family.

**If you fail once, try again!** The first dinner failed because Abu Lahab broke it up. But the Prophet didn't give up — he tried again and again.

**Insults are not the end.** Abu Lahab insulted the Prophet in front of everyone. But the Prophet didn't insult back. He stayed calm and continued his mission. What extraordinary character!
