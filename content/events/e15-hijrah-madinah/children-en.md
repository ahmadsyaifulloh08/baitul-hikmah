# The Hijrah to Madinah 🌙

---

## The Evil Plan of the Quraysh

The Quraysh were furious that so many Muslims had moved to Madinah. They gathered and made an evil plan: to kill Prophet Muhammad ﷺ! Young men from every clan would attack the Prophet all at once.

But Allah told the Prophet about their plan. That night, the Prophet asked his cousin **Ali ibn Abi Talib** to sleep in his bed wearing his green cloak. The Quraysh youth surrounding the house thought the Prophet was still sleeping inside!

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ walked right past them **without being seen**, reciting verses of the Quran. Allah covered their eyes so that no one noticed the Prophet had left. Subhanallah!

## Hiding in the Cave of Thawr

The Prophet went to the home of his best friend, **Abu Bakr al-Siddiq**. They set off together — but not northward toward Madinah! Instead they went south, to the **Cave of Thawr**, so the Quraysh would search in the wrong direction. Very clever!

Inside the cave, Abu Bakr cleaned out every hole that might hide snakes. One hole was left, and Abu Bakr sealed it with **his own heel**. A snake bit his heel, but Abu Bakr didn't move because he didn't want to wake the Prophet sleeping in his lap. That's a true friend! 💪

The most thrilling moment came when the Quraysh trackers arrived at the cave entrance. Abu Bakr whispered in fear, but the Prophet said calmly: **"Do not grieve; indeed Allah is with us."**

Allah protected them — the trackers never entered the cave!

## The Chaser Who Changed His Mind

After three days, the Prophet and Abu Bakr set off for Madinah along the coastal route. The Quraysh offered a reward of **100 camels** to anyone who captured the Prophet — that's a huge amount!

A skilled tracker named **Suraqah ibn Malik** chased after them. But every time his horse got close, it **sank into the sand**! Once, twice, three times — his horse kept sinking. Suraqah realised that a power was protecting the Prophet.

Suraqah stopped chasing and asked for forgiveness. The Prophet even promised him: "One day, you will wear the bracelets of the King of Persia!" Suraqah must have thought, "That's impossible!" But years later, that promise **actually came true**! 😲

## Welcome to Madinah!

The people of Madinah had been waiting for the Prophet every single day! They went out each morning to the edge of the city, waited until the sun got too hot, then went home and waited again the next day.

Finally, the day they were waiting for arrived! When Prophet Muhammad ﷺ was spotted in the distance, the whole of Madinah **erupted with joy**. The young girls of the Ansar sang: *"The full moon has risen upon us..."* 🎶

The Prophet built **Masjid Quba** — the very first mosque in Islam — in the first village he stopped at.

The hijrah was so important that it became the **start of the Islamic calendar** (the Hijri calendar). Every time we see a Hijri year, we remember this incredible journey!

**An important lesson:** The hijrah teaches us that **Allah always protects those who believe**, as long as we also try our very best. The Prophet planned everything carefully, then placed his trust in Allah. That is true *tawakkul*! 🌟
