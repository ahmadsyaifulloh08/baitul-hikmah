# The Hijrah to Madinah 🌙

## 🌍 What Happened?

The bad people in Makkah didn't want Prophet Muhammad ﷺ to teach about Islam anymore. They even planned to **hurt** him! But Allah had a better plan — Allah told the Prophet to move to a new city called **Madinah**.

## 📖 The Story...

One night, the bad people surrounded Prophet Muhammad's house. They wanted to catch him! But the Prophet had a clever plan:

He asked his cousin, **Ali**, to sleep in his bed and wear his green cloak. Brave Ali agreed! 🛡️

Then Prophet Muhammad slipped out of the house in the dark night. And guess what? Allah made the bad people's eyes **unable to see** him! Subhanallah! ✨

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ traveled with his best friend, **Abu Bakr**. They hid in a cave called **Cave Thawr**. There, Allah sent a **spider** to spin its web across the cave entrance, and a **dove** to build its nest there — so the bad people thought the cave was empty!

Abu Bakr was very scared and whispered: *"They are so close!"*

But Prophet Muhammad smiled calmly and said: **"Do not grieve, Allah is with us."** 🤲

After three days, they continued their journey to Madinah. And when they arrived... wow! All the people of Madinah came out to welcome them, singing:

> 🎵 *"The full moon has risen upon us..."* 🎵

They were so happy! 🌟

## 🌟 Lessons We Can Learn...

1. **Be brave and smart** — The Prophet didn't just run away. He planned everything carefully, but still **trusted in Allah**.
2. **True friendship** — Abu Bakr stayed with the Prophet in danger. A true friend is someone who's there when times are tough!
3. **Allah always protects** — A tiny spider can become a protector if Allah wills it. Nothing is too small to be Allah's help!

## 🤲 Prayer & Verses

Allah says about the moment in Cave Thawr:

> *"Do not grieve; indeed Allah is with us."*
> — Qur'an, Al-Tawbah: 40

Whenever you feel scared or alone, remember: **Allah is always with you!** 💕
