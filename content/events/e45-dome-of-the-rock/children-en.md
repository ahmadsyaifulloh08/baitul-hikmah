# The Dome of the Rock — The Golden Dome in Jerusalem ✨

---

## The Holy City of Jerusalem

**Jerusalem** (called *al-Quds* in Arabic) is a very special city for Muslims. It was from this city that Prophet Muhammad ﷺ made his miraculous night journey — the **Isra Mi'raj** — ascending to the heavens to receive the command for the five daily prayers.

In Jerusalem, there is a sacred hill called the **Haram al-Sharif**. On top of that hill sits a great rock with deep historical significance — this is the rock from which Prophet Muhammad ﷺ ascended to the heavens. This rock is also important in the story of Prophet Ibrahim and the prophets before him.

## Caliph Abd al-Malik Had a Grand Plan

Around 685 CE, Caliph **Abd al-Malik ibn Marwan** decided to build a magnificent structure over that sacred rock. He wanted to create something the world had never seen before!

Abd al-Malik sent two of the finest builders: **Raja' ibn Haywah** and **Yazid ibn Salam**. To pay for this enormous project, he used all the tax revenue from Egypt for **seven years**! Imagine how big this project was.

Workers and artisans came from every corner of the Muslim world. They brought the finest materials — gold, colorful mosaics, marble, and beautiful stones.

## One of the Most Beautiful Buildings in the World

After years of hard work, in **691 CE**, the Qubbat al-Sakhrah (*Dome of the Rock*) was finally completed! And the result? Absolutely breathtaking!

The building is **octagonal** in shape with a great golden dome on top that gleams in the sunlight. Its walls are decorated with **mosaics** in gold, blue, and green forming beautiful plant patterns. Around the interior walls, there are **Quranic verses** inscribed along a band stretching 240 meters!

Most amazingly — this building still stands today, more than **1,300 years** later! Its golden dome still shines and has become a symbol of Jerusalem recognized across the entire world.

## Lessons from the Dome of the Rock

The story of building the Dome of the Rock teaches us:

- **Art can be worship** — Muslim artists used beauty to honor a holy place and convey the message of the Quran.
- **Patience brings results** — It took years to complete this building, but the result has lasted over a thousand years!
- **Islam honors all prophets** — This building was built on a site connected to Prophet Ibrahim, Prophet Sulayman, and Prophet Muhammad ﷺ, showing that Islam respects all prophets.
- **Beauty unites** — The Dome of the Rock is admired by people from all over the world, proving that beauty can be a bridge between people.
