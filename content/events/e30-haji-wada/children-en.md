# The Farewell Pilgrimage 🕋

---

## The Great Journey to Makkah

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ announced that he would go on Hajj — the pilgrimage to Makkah. This was his first and last Hajj — though nobody knew it at the time.

The news spread across all of Arabia. People came from everywhere — from the deserts, mountains, and coasts. **More than a hundred thousand people** joined the Prophet ﷺ! Imagine — a sea of people as far as the eye could see, all chanting: *"Labbaik Allahumma labbaik!"* — "Here I am at Your service, O Allah!"

They all walked together toward Makkah, the holy city where the Ka'bah stands. The journey was filled with joy and gratitude.

## Standing on the Plain of Arafah

The most important part of Hajj is **standing at Arafah** — standing on the vast plain while praying to Allah. The Prophet ﷺ said: **"Hajj is Arafah."**

On this enormous plain, mounted on his camel named al-Qaswa', Prophet Muhammad ﷺ delivered his **final sermon** — a speech of great importance for all of humanity.

More than a hundred thousand people listened. They stood in silence, their hearts full of attention. What the Prophet ﷺ said that day is still remembered now — more than 1,400 years later!

On that same day, Allah revealed a very special verse: **"This day I have perfected for you your religion."** (QS Al-Ma'idah: 3)

## The Important Messages in the Sermon

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ delivered many important messages. Here are some of them:

🩸 **Don't hurt others** — "Your blood, your property, and your honor are sacred — it is forbidden to harm them!" This means: we must not hurt anyone, must not steal, and must not insult others.

👩 **Treat women well** — "Be good to women!" The Prophet ﷺ reminded everyone that women have the right to be treated with respect and kindness.

🌈 **All people are equal** — "There is no superiority of an Arab over a non-Arab, nor of a white-skinned person over a dark-skinned person — except through piety." This means: what makes a person noble is not their skin color or tribe, but the goodness of their heart!

📖 **Hold on to the Quran** — "I have left for you the Book of Allah. If you hold fast to it, you will never go astray."

## Lessons from the Farewell Hajj

The final sermon of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ teaches us things that are very important for all of us:

⚖️ **Justice for everyone** — Every person has equal rights, no matter where they come from.

🤝 **Respect and care for others** — Never hurt anyone, whether with actions or words.

👧 **Honor women** — Islam teaches that women must be treated with kindness and respect.

🌍 **All humans are brothers and sisters** — Skin color, language, or tribe does not determine a person's worth. What matters is the goodness of their heart.

Imagine — these messages were delivered more than **1,400 years ago**, but they are still deeply needed in our world today. That is the greatness of Prophet Muhammad's teachings!
