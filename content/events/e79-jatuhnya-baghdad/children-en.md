
# The Fall of Baghdad 😢

## ⚔️ Why Did This Happen?

Baghdad was once the **most beautiful and smartest city in the world**. It had Bayt al-Hikmah, a library full of books from all over the world. Scientists from many countries came there to learn and discover new things.

But sadly, Muslims at that time were **not united**. Their leaders were fighting each other and wouldn't work together. And from the east came a very large and powerful army — the **Mongols** led by **Hulagu Khan**! ⚡

## 🛡️ How Did It Happen?

Hulagu Khan brought a massive army — over **100,000 soldiers**! They surrounded Baghdad and attacked its walls.

Caliph al-Musta'sim, the leader of Baghdad, was sadly not prepared. He hadn't built a strong army and wouldn't listen to warnings. 😔

After a few weeks, Baghdad's walls fell. And what happened next was very, very sad:

- 📚 Books from the libraries were **thrown into the Tigris River** — so many that the river water **turned black** from all the ink!
- 🏛️ Mosques, schools, and hospitals were **destroyed**
- Many, many people lost their lives

Imagine — all the knowledge collected over **hundreds of years**, lost in just days! 💔

## 🕊️ What Happened After...

But do you know something amazing? Even though Baghdad was destroyed, **Islam was not destroyed**!

In fact, many Mongol soldiers later **became Muslim**! Those who came to destroy were **attracted by the beauty of Islam** and became Muslims themselves!

This proves that the strength of Islam is not just in buildings or books — but in the **hearts of people who believe**. ❤️

The center of Islamic civilization moved to other places: Egypt, Turkey, and India. And the knowledge that had already spread around the world **could not be destroyed**!

## 💪 Values We Can Learn

1. **Unity matters!** — Baghdad fell because Muslims were divided. When we're united, we're stronger!
2. **Knowledge must be protected** — Don't waste your chance to learn. The knowledge in your brain can never be taken from you!
3. **Never give up** — Even after losing so much, Muslims rose again and continued building civilization!

Allah says:

> *"Indeed, with hardship comes ease."*
> — Qur'an, Al-Insyirah: 6

Even after the greatest sadness, there is always hope! 🌅
