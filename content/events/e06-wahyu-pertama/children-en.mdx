# The First Revelation at Cave Hira ✨

## 🌍 What Happened?

On a very quiet night, inside a small cave on top of a mountain called **Jabal Nur** (the Mountain of Light), Prophet Muhammad ﷺ was sitting alone. He often went to this cave to think and pray. The cave was called **Cave Hira**, and from there, you could see the Ka'bah far away in the distance.

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ was 40 years old at that time. He was known as the most honest and kindest person in all of Makkah. Everyone called him **"Al-Amin"** — which means **"The Trustworthy."**

## 📖 The Story...

Suddenly, the Angel Jibril (Gabriel) appeared! Jibril was a very great and powerful angel. He said to Prophet Muhammad:

**"Iqra'!"** — which means: **"Read!"**

Prophet Muhammad replied: *"I cannot read."*

Then Jibril hugged him very, very tightly — like the strongest hug ever — and said again: **"Iqra'!"**

This happened three times! And on the third time, Jibril recited the very first verses of the Qur'an:

> *"Read in the name of your Lord who created..."*

Wow! This was **the beginning of the Qur'an** coming down to Earth! 📖✨

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ came down from the mountain shaking and trembling. He went home and said to his wife Khadijah: *"Cover me! Cover me!"*

Khadijah — who was very loving and wise — held him and said: *"Don't worry, Allah will never let you down. You are kind, you help people, and you are honest!"*

## 🌟 Lessons We Can Learn...

Do you know what the coolest thing is? The very first command from Allah to humanity was: **"READ!"** 📚

Not a command to fight. Not a command to build palaces. But a command to **learn and read!**

This means Allah really loves people who love to learn. Every time you read a book, do your homework, or learn something new — you are following Allah's very first command! How cool is that? 😊

And here's another lesson: when Prophet Muhammad ﷺ was scared, the first person he went to was his family. Khadijah didn't say "oh, you're just dreaming" — she **believed in him and supported him**. That's what an amazing family looks like! 💕

## 🤲 Prayer & Verses

Allah says:

> *"Read in the name of your Lord who created."*
> — Qur'an, Al-'Alaq: 1

And Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said:

> *"Seeking knowledge is an obligation upon every Muslim."*
> — Narrated by Ibn Majah (Hasan)

So every time you study — remember that you are following Allah's very first command! 🌟
