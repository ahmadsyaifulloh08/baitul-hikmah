# Three Years in the Lonely Valley

## 🏔️ Driven into a Narrow Valley

At one time in the city of Mecca, the Quraysh leaders felt very angry. **Prophet Muhammad SAW** continued to convey goodness and invite people to worship Allah alone. More and more people were following him each day.

The Quraysh leaders gathered and made an evil plan. They wrote an agreement on a sheet of leather and hung it inside the Ka'bah. Its contents: no one was allowed to trade, marry, or even speak with the family of **Abu Talib** — the uncle of Prophet Muhammad who always protected him.

So the entire Banu Hashim family had to move to a small, narrow valley on the outskirts of Mecca. The valley was called *Shi'b Abi Talib*. There they lived — far from the market, far from neighbors, far from normal life.

## 🍞 Very Hard Days

The Banu Hashim family could not go to the market to buy bread. Could not buy rice, milk, or fruit. Even no one would sell drinking water to them. This is what the Banu Hashim family experienced for nearly three years!

Small children cried from hunger. The sound of their crying could be heard outside the valley. But the Quraysh leaders who passed by only smiled when they heard it. They felt no compassion at all.

**Sa'd ibn Abi Waqqas**, one of the Prophet's companions, once told of how one night he found a piece of dried camel hide on the ground. Because he was so hungry, he washed the hide, burned it, ground it up, then mixed it with water and ate it. That camel hide became his food for three days! That's how hard their life was.

## 🌙 Secret Help

Not all Quraysh people agreed with this cruelty. There were good people who secretly helped!

**Hisham ibn Amr**, a brave man, every night loaded his camel with food. Then he would release the camel toward the valley mouth so the Banu Hashim family could take the food. He did this repeatedly, even though it was very dangerous for him if discovered.

**Hakim ibn Hizam**, the nephew of **Khadijah** (Prophet Muhammad's wife), also smuggled wheat for his aunt. He didn't care about the threats from the Quraysh leaders. For him, letting his family starve was something he could not accept.

Allah saw all that goodness. Every grain of food sent secretly, every prayer offered in the quiet night — none of it was ever in vain.

## 📜 Termites and Miracle

After nearly three years, five kind-hearted Quraysh people dared to speak up. They stood in front of the Ka'bah and declared: "This is unjust! How can we eat well while the children of Banu Hashim are starving?"

Then a miracle happened! When the evil agreement sheet was taken from inside the Ka'bah, it turned out that small termites had eaten all the writing! What remained was only one sentence: *"Bismika Allahumma"* — which means "In Your name, O Allah."

Prophet Muhammad SAW already knew this would happen before the sheet was opened, because Allah told him through revelation. Subhanallah! Allah always protects those who are patient and faithful.

Finally, the boycott was cancelled. The Banu Hashim family could leave that narrow valley and return to normal life. Three full years of patience had ended. But they emerged with faith stronger than before!
