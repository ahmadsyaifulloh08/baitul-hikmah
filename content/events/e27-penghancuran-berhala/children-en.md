# The Destruction of Idols at the Ka'bah 🕋✨

---

## 🌍 What Was the Ka'bah Like Before?

Do you know who built the Ka'bah? **Prophet Ibrahim** and his son **Prophet Ismail** *'alayhimas salam*! They built it as a house of worship for **Allah alone**.

But over hundreds of years, the people of Arabia forgot the Ka'bah's true purpose. Little by little, they brought statues and idols into the Ka'bah. They started worshipping these idols and forgot about Allah.

Eventually, there were **360 idols** in and around the Ka'bah! Some were made of stone, some of wood. The biggest one was called **Hubal** — made of red stone and shaped like a person. Imagine — a house built for Allah, filled with statues!

## 🕋 The Great Cleansing!

When Prophet Muhammad ﷺ liberated Makkah, the first thing he did was go to the Ka'bah.

He circled the Ka'bah seven times on his camel. In his hand was a staff. Each time his staff pointed at an idol — **the idol fell!** Some fell flat on their faces, some tumbled backwards. One by one, all 360 idols came crashing down!

While doing this, Prophet Muhammad ﷺ recited a verse from the Quran: **"Truth has come and falsehood has vanished. Indeed, falsehood is ever bound to vanish!"**

After that, all the idols were taken out and destroyed. The stone ones were smashed. The wooden ones were burned. The Ka'bah was cleaned of every image and statue.

Finally, the Ka'bah was clean and pure again — just as it was when Prophet Ibrahim first built it!

## 🔊 Bilal's Voice from the Top of the Ka'bah

After the Ka'bah was cleansed, a very special moment happened.

Prophet Muhammad ﷺ chose **Bilal ibn Rabah** to climb to the top of the Ka'bah and call the *adhan* — the very first call to prayer ever heard from atop the Ka'bah!

Who was Bilal? He was a **former slave** from Abyssinia (present-day Ethiopia). He had been tortured by his master for believing in Allah. A huge boulder was placed on his chest under the burning sun. But Bilal stayed strong and only said: **"Ahad! Ahad!"** (The One! The One!)

And now... the man who was once tortured stood at the **highest and most noble place** in the entire world, calling out the name of Allah! His voice echoed across all of Makkah.

This is one of Islam's most powerful messages: **no person is better than another because of their skin color, wealth, or family name**. The most honored is the one with the strongest faith and best character.

## 💡 What Can We Learn?

- **Restore things to their true purpose** 🏠 — The Ka'bah was built to worship Allah. When it was polluted, it had to be cleaned. Our hearts are the same — they should always be clean for Allah.

- **Truth always wins** ✨ — For hundreds of years, idols stood in the Ka'bah. But in the end, truth came and swept them all away. Be patient and trust in Allah!

- **All people are equal** 🤝 — Bilal, who was once a slave, stood at the highest point of the Ka'bah. Islam teaches that all people are equal — what matters is their faith and their goodness.

- **Prophet Muhammad continued Prophet Ibrahim's mission** 🔗 — Prophet Ibrahim built the Ka'bah, Prophet Muhammad purified it. Both shared the same mission: teaching monotheism!
