# Image Briefs — Children Slideshow (51 Unique Illustrations)

> **LARANGAN MUTLAK — Visualisasi Nabi Muhammad ﷺ:**
> TIDAK BOLEH menampilkan fisik Nabi dalam bentuk APAPUN — termasuk siluet tubuh, sosok dari belakang, anak kecil, bayangan.
> Yang BOLEH: cahaya/nur keemasan (bukan bentuk manusia), simbol (bintang, sinar), objek (tongkat, sandal — tanpa sosok).

> **Style**: Warm watercolor storybook, children age 6-12, **16:9 landscape (1792×1024)**, soft edges, stylized (not photorealistic). Arabian 6th century. Sandy gold, warm beige, dusty earth.
> **RATIO**: Wajib 16:9 landscape. Prompt HARUS include: `"Image ratio: exactly 16:9 landscape (wide). Resolution: 1792x1024 or similar."`
> **SETIAP slide = gambar UNIK** — tidak boleh ada duplikat.
> **NO TEXT**: Gambar TIDAK BOLEH mengandung text/huruf/caption/signature apapun.
> **SAFE ZONE**: Semua subjek utama (wajah, kepala, karakter, objek kunci) WAJIB di CENTER 70% area gambar. Edge/pinggir harus bersih dari elemen penting — akan ter-crop di berbagai ukuran layar. Gambar ditampilkan fullscreen `object-fit: cover`.
> **CAHAYA MUHAMMAD ﷺ (KONSISTEN)**: Kehadiran Nabi Muhammad ﷺ direpresentasikan sebagai **cahaya emas bersinar (golden radiant glow)** — lingkaran cahaya emas yang bersinar ke segala arah dengan sinar-sinar lembut (rays). Warna: warm golden-white. Ukuran: proporsional dengan scene (~15-20% tinggi gambar). WAJIB konsisten di SEMUA slide — bentuk, warna, ukuran sama. Tidak boleh variasi (column, ribbon, burst, starburst dll) — SELALU golden circular glow with rays.
> **NO BLACK BORDERS**: Gambar HARUS mengisi canvas 100% edge-to-edge. Tidak boleh ada black bars, letterbox, atau padding kosong.

## Character Consistency Reference
- **Abrahah**: Yemeni king, ornate red/gold robe, short dark beard, golden crown, arrogant posture
- **Gajah Mahmud**: Largest war elephant, dark gray, red war saddle with gold cloth, much bigger than other elephants
- **Abdul Muthalib**: Elderly dignified Arab leader, white beard, clean white robe, calm noble posture
- **Halimah**: Bedouin woman, simple brown/cream clothing, head covering, kind gentle appearance
- **Ummu Aiman**: Dark-skinned woman (Ethiopian), simple modest clothing, protective/caring demeanor
- **Abu Thalib**: Middle-aged Arab man, modest brown/beige robe, kind face, not wealthy appearance
- **Ka'bah**: Simple stone cube draped in cloth, no ornate decoration, desert setting
- **Young Muhammad (12 yo)**: ⚠️ CANNOT be depicted. Use golden radiant glow only. Context clues (camel, staff, position) indicate presence.
- **Bahira**: Elderly Christian monk, long white beard, dark brown/black monastic robes, wise gentle eyes, Busra monastery setting
- **Maysarah**: Khadijah's servant, middle-aged Arab man, simple traveler clothing, loyal appearance
- **Khadijah bint Khuwaylid**: Elegant businesswoman, **white hijab and niqab/cadar** (face veil, only eyes visible), neat clean clothing (green/white), dignified posture, known as al-Tahirah (the Pure)
- **Nafisah**: Khadijah's friend, modest Arab woman, warm approachable face

## E01 — Tahun Gajah (12 slides)

### e01-slide-03 — Pasukan gajah bersiap berangkat

### e01-slide-04 — Pasukan berjalan melintasi padang pasir
Long marching column crossing desert LEFT TO RIGHT (toward Makkah). Mahmud leads at front, trunk raised. Behind him: smaller elephants, cavalry, infantry with banners. Dust clouds rising. Hijaz hills in distance. Tiny silhouette of Makkah far right on horizon. Mood: overwhelming military power.

### e01-slide-05 — Pasukan mengambil unta di dekat Makkah
Soldiers seizing 200 camels from a grazing field near Makkah. Camels being herded away by soldiers. In the background, the outskirts of Makkah visible. A few Arab herders watch helplessly. Mood: aggression, theft.

### e01-slide-06 — Abdul Muthalib menemui Abrahah di tenda
Inside ornate military tent. Abdul Muthalib (white robe, white beard, standing tall and calm) faces Abrahah (seated on throne, surrounded by guards, looking surprised/confused). Between them: tension. Through tent opening: camels visible outside. Mood: dignity vs power.

### e01-slide-07 — "Ka'bah punya Tuhan yang akan melindunginya"
Close-up moment: Abdul Muthalib's hand raised in a calm gesture of certainty. Abrahah leaning forward in disbelief. Light coming through tent opening illuminating Abdul Muthalib. Mood: unshakeable faith. (Different angle/composition from slide 06)

### e01-slide-08 — Gajah Mahmud berlutut menolak maju
Mahmud the elephant KNEELING on the ground, front legs folded, head bowed LOW. He FACES TOWARD Ka'bah (visible in the distance ahead of him) but REFUSES to move. Handler on top whipping desperately but elephant won't budge. Other elephants behind also stopping, confused. Mood: divine intervention.
⚠️ CRITICAL: Elephant faces TOWARD Ka'bah but kneels/refuses. Ka'bah is AHEAD of elephant, not behind.

### e01-slide-09 — Burung ababil datang dari langit
Sky FILLED with hundreds of small dark birds (ababil) flying in formation from above. Each bird carries 3 small glowing reddish stones (1 in beak, 1 in each claw). Stones raining down on panicking soldiers below. Dramatic sky — dark storm on one side, golden light over Ka'bah on other side. Mood: divine punishment, awe-inspiring.

### e01-slide-10 — Kehancuran pasukan Abrahah
Aftermath scene: abandoned military equipment scattered on desert ground — broken saddles, torn banners, empty armor. No dead bodies shown (children-friendly). Dust settling. In the distance, Ka'bah stands untouched, bathed in golden light. A few birds still circling in sky. Mood: aftermath, Ka'bah protected.

### e01-slide-11 — Malam kelahiran Nabi Muhammad ﷺ
Night scene in Makkah. A simple stone house with WARM GOLDEN LIGHT emanating from within — glowing through windows and door, spreading outward. Night sky filled with extraordinarily bright stars, one brilliant star directly above the house. Crescent moon. Silhouette of Makkah rooftops, Ka'bah faintly visible. A few women (from behind) walking toward the house.
⚠️ NO baby, NO human figure representing the Prophet. Only LIGHT from inside the house.

### e01-slide-12 — Tahun Gajah sebagai penanda waktu
Symbolic/artistic composition: Ka'bah at center, protected by a dome of golden light. Around it: remnants of broken elephant armor (past), bright star above (birth), and radiating timeline markers spreading outward. Desert landscape stretching to horizon. Dawn sky. Mood: significance, legacy, hope.
