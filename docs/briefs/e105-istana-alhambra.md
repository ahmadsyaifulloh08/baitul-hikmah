## Base Palette

- Golden-blue interior, intricate geometric patterns, warm lighting

- Brown wood, scholarly colors, warm lamp light
- Tourist colors: varied clothing, UNESCO blue, camera equipment

- **Court Scholars**: Various ages wearing traditional scholar robes in earth tones, carrying manuscripts and writing materials

- **International Visitors**: Modern tourists from various nationalities with cameras and guidebooks
