# E102 — Masjid Samarra (12 slides)

## Base Palette
- Brown brick, green gardens, blue river, golden domes
- Construction colors: brown wood scaffolding, red brick, white mortar, blue worker clothing
- Golden glory vs faded brown modern, blue modern technology
- Green scholar robes, golden astronomical tools, blue night, white stars

## Character Lock
- **Khalifah Al-Mutawakkil**: A dignified Arab ruler about 45 with a WELL-GROOMED BLACK BEARD with some grey. He wears RICH BLACK/GOLD KHALIFAH ROBES with ornate patterns. Commanding presence, architectural patron
- **Khalifah Al-Mutasim**: Founder of Samarra, a strong military ruler about 40 with a THICK BLACK BEARD. He wears DARK BLUE/GOLD ROBES with military insignia. Visionary city builder
- **Construction Workers**: Mixed ethnicities working on massive construction, wearing simple working clothes and turbans
- **Muslim Scientist**: A scholar wearing GREEN ROBES with astronomical instruments and manuscripts
