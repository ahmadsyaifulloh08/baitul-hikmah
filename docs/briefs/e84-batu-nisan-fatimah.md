## Slides

### slide-01 — Fatimah sakitnya menjelang wafat
Close-up interior shot of simple Medina home. Fatimah resting on modest bedding, appearing weak but spiritually radiant. Ali caring for her tenderly. Hassan and Hussain nearby with worried expressions. Warm golden light filtering through window suggests divine presence. Colors: brown clay walls, white bedding, green Ali's robe, gold spiritual light, cream children's clothing. Mood: illness, family love, spiritual preparation.

### slide-02 — Pemakaman Fatimah secara rahasia
Medium shot of quiet nighttime burial in Baqi cemetery. Small group including Ali and few close companions burying Fatimah according to her wishes. No large crowd, very private ceremony respecting her request. Medina sleeping in background. Colors: blue night sky, white burial shroud, brown earth, gray cemetery stones, silver moonlight. Mood: private grief, respectful farewell, following her wishes.

### slide-03 — Ali tidak menunjukkan lokasi makam persis
Wide shot of Ali walking through Baqi cemetery with Hassan and Hussain, deliberately not marking exact burial location. Multiple similar graves visible. Ali honoring Fatimah's request for privacy even in death. Simple unmarked burial plot among others. Colors: gray cemetery stones, brown earth paths, green Ali's robe, white children's clothing, blue peaceful sky. Mood: mystery honored, humble burial, respectful secrecy.

### slide-04 — Ziarah modern ke area pemakaman Fatimah
Establishing shot of contemporary pilgrims visiting Baqi cemetery area in Medina. Modern Muslim families paying respects in general area where Fatimah may be buried. Exact location still unknown, but spiritual connection maintained. Simple prayer and remembrance. Colors: white modern pilgrimage clothing, brown historical cemetery, green Islamic banners, gold spiritual reverence, blue sky of continuity. Mood: enduring respect, mystery preserved, spiritual connection across centuries.
