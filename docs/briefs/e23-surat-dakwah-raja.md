## Base Palette
- Warm scribal brown, cream parchment, golden seal
- World map blues and greens with messenger routes
- Royal courts with various cultural colors and grandeur
- Contrasting reactions across four kingdoms

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.
- **Messengers**: Various companions chosen as diplomatic envoys to different kingdoms
