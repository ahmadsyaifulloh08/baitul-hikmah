# E103 — Qutb Minar (11 slides)

## Base Palette
- Green banners, white turbans, golden weapons, blue sky
- Red brick, brown wood, white marble, blue sky
- White marble, gold calligraphy, blue-green Islamic arches
- Golden hour light, warm earth tones, multicultural harmony

## Character Lock
- **Qutb-ud-din Aibak**: A determined Muslim general about 40 with a WELL-GROOMED BLACK BEARD. He wears GREEN MILITARY ROBES with gold trim and WHITE TURBAN. Strong leadership presence, architectural patron
- **Iltutmish**: A wise sultan about 45 with a TRIMMED GREY-BLACK BEARD. He wears ROYAL BLUE ROBES with silver embroidery and a jeweled turban. Completion visionary, refined ruler
- **Construction Workers**: Mixed Muslim and local Indian workers in simple clothing and turbans
- **Architects**: Both Muslim and Hindu craftsmen collaborating, wearing traditional robes of their cultures
