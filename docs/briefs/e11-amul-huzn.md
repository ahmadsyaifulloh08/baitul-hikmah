## Base Palette
- Warm brown-gold interior light, soft shadows
- Green nature, cream-white clothing, morning gold
- Purple sunset, red-brown blood traces, golden hope
- Deep blue starry night, golden divine light

## Character Lock
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form, no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst
- **Abu Thalib**: An elderly dignified Arab man about 50-55 with a MEDIUM-LENGTH DARK BROWN BEARD with grey streaks. He wears WARM BROWN ROBES and a LIGHT TAN KEFFIYEH. LEAN build, kind face with wrinkles, tired but still protective. Lying sick in bed
- **Khadijah**: A dignified Arab woman about 65 with gentle aged beauty. She wears a HIJAB MATCHING HER CLOTHING COLOR and modest CREAM/WHITE FLOWING ROBES. Graceful posture, wise maternal expression, generous hands
- **Abu Jahl**: A harsh-faced Arab man about 45 with a THICK BLACK BEARD. He wears DARK RED/MAROON ROBES. Cruel eyes, aggressive posture, menacing presence
