## Base Palette
- Baghdad gold domes and prosperity colors
- Black Mongol threat clouds and banners
- Tigris river blues, destruction grays, fire reds

## Character Lock
- **Khalifah al-Musta'sim**: Last Abbasid caliph in fine robes with turban, unprepared expression
- **Hulagu Khan**: Mongol commander in fur-trimmed armor, fierce commanding presence, dark hair
- **Muslim scholars**: Men in scholarly robes carrying books and scrolls, worried faces
- **Mongol warriors**: Horsemen in leather armor, fur caps, curved bows, fierce expressions

## Slides

### slide-01 — Baghdad masa kejayaan kontras dengan ancaman Mongol
Split composition establishing shot. FOREGROUND: Golden Baghdad with Baitul Hikmah dome, scholars with books, peaceful prosperity. BACKGROUND: Dark storm clouds from east with Mongol cavalry silhouettes approaching. Colors: gold Baghdad domes, white scholar robes, brown manuscripts, black threatening clouds, red Mongol banners. Mood: prosperity meeting danger.

### slide-02 — Sungai Tigris hitam karena tinta buku yang dibuang
Medium shot of Tigris River turned black from ink of thousands of books thrown in water. Mongol soldiers dumping manuscripts from Baitul Hikmah into river. Burning buildings in background. Books floating and sinking in dark water. Colors: black ink-stained water, red flames, brown book pages, gray smoke, white manuscript pages scattered. Mood: cultural destruction, tragedy of lost knowledge.

### slide-03 — Tentara Mongol yang kemudian masuk Islam
Close-up transformation scene. Mongol warriors removing helmets and bowing in Islamic prayer posture, guided by Muslim scholars. Former enemies now united in prayer. Peaceful atmosphere after violence. Colors: brown prayer rugs, white Islamic robes, green spiritual light, gold peaceful illumination. Mood: conversion, spiritual transformation, hope from despair.

### slide-04 — Pusat peradaban Islam baru di Cairo, Istanbul, Delhi
Wide shot map-style view of new Islamic centers rising. Three cities with glowing mosques and libraries: Cairo with Mamluk architecture, Istanbul with Ottoman domes, Delhi with Indo-Islamic style. Scholars traveling between cities carrying preserved knowledge. Colors: gold mosque domes, green Islamic banners, blue trade routes, white scholar convoys. Mood: resilience, renaissance, knowledge preservation.
