## Base Palette
- Sandy gold, white stone, bright blue sky
- Transition from dark/grey to golden/white

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.
- **Bilal**: A young man about 30 with DARK BROWN SKIN Ethiopian. Strong build despite hardship. He wears SIMPLE WHITE/OFF-WHITE CLOTHING initially torn/worn as slave, later clean. Bright determined eyes, dignified posture even in suffering.
