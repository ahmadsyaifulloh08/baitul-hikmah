## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)

## Slides

### slide-01 — Ka'bah dipenuhi 360 berhala sebelum Islam
Wide establishing shot of ancient Mecca. The Ka'bah black cubic structure surrounded by 360 various idols and statues of different sizes and materials. Stone, wood, metal statues scattered around. Hubal the largest red stone idol prominent. Morning light. Cluttered sacred space.

### slide-02 — Berhala-berhala jatuh roboh saat ditunjuk
Medium shot of Ka'bah with multiple idols falling down and breaking on the ground. Stone fragments, wooden pieces scattered. Empty spaces where idols used to stand. Dramatic lighting with shadows. Transformation from cluttered to cleaner space gradually happening.

### slide-03 — Bilal mengumandangkan adzan pertama di atas Ka'bah
Low angle shot looking up. Bilal standing tall on top of Ka'bah, hands cupped to ears performing adzan call to prayer. Golden hour sunlight behind him creating warm silhouette. Crowd of people below looking up with reverence. Clear blue sky background. Triumphant moment.

### slide-04 — Perbandingan sebelum dan sesudah pembersihan
Split composition comparing before and after. LEFT: Ka'bah surrounded by many dark idols, gloomy atmosphere. RIGHT: Clean pristine Ka'bah standing alone, bright golden light illuminating it. Clear dramatic contrast between dark past and bright pure future. Transformation complete.
