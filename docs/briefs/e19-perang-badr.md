## Base Palette
- Dramatic contrast: dark storm clouds, bright armor banners
- Desert tan, blue sky, tension before battle
- Battle dust, golden divine assistance, courage over violence
- Victory celebration with gratitude, not violence

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.
- **Hamzah ibn Abdul Muttalib**: Large muscular Arab man with thick dark beard. He wears lion skin over brown robes. Broad shoulders, fierce protective expression. Known as Lion of Allah
- **Ali ibn Abi Talib**: Young brave Arab man with short dark beard. He wears olive green robes and white turban. Athletic compact build, youthful intense eyes, courageous expression
- **Abu Bakr al-Siddiq**: Slim gentle-looking Arab man about 38 years old with light brown reddish beard medium length. He wears white robes and white turban. Soft features, kind eyes, compassionate expression
- **Muslim soldiers**: Soldiers in simple armor leather chainmail over earth-toned robes. Round shields, straight swords, spears. Green or white banners with no text. Determined faces
- **Quraisy army**: Much larger force with more elaborate equipment, representing overwhelming odds
