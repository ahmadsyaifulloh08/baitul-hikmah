## Base Palette
- Dark blue night with golden stars and hope
- Warm golden brown clay mosque with movement
- Contrasting grey doubt vs bright yellow relief
- Green and blue world unity with joyful brotherhood

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.
- **Praying congregation**: Rows of Muslim worshippers in various simple robes
