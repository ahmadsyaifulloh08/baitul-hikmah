## Base Palette
- Brown earth, green date palms, blue sky
- Warm earth brown, blue sky, cheerful building
- Warm green, golden hues, brotherhood warmth
- Cream warm, green shade, peaceful blessed atmosphere

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.
- **Al-Qashwa camel**: Beloved camel of the Prophet, kneeling down to select the location
- **Abu Bakr al-Siddiq**: Slim gentle-looking Arab man about 38 years old with light brown reddish beard medium length. He wears white robes and white turban. Soft features, kind eyes, compassionate expression
- **Abdurrahman bin Auf**: Dignified Muhajirin merchant, humble expression, simple travel-worn robes
- **Sad bin al-Rabi**: Generous Anshari man in fine Madinah robes, open-palmed giving gesture
- **Abu Hurairah**: Young eager student with earnest face, always close to the Prophet to memorize hadith
