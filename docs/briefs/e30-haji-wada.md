## Base Palette
- White ihram clothing, golden sunset, brown desert
- Warm earth tones with divine golden light

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.

## Slides

### slide-01 — Seratus ribu jamaah berjalan menuju Makkah
Panoramic wide shot of massive crowd wearing white ihram clothes walking across desert landscape toward Makkah in distance. Mountains in background, Ka'bah visible as small dark cube on horizon. Golden sunset light illuminating the procession. Epic scale pilgrimage scene.

### slide-02 — Khutbah di Padang Arafah yang dipenuhi jemaah
Bird's eye aerial view of vast Arafat plain completely filled with pilgrims in white clothing. GOLDEN GLOW positioned at center where Prophet delivered sermon. Dramatic golden hour sky above. Massive gathering showing scale of historic moment. Sacred atmosphere.

### slide-03 — Gulungan perkamen dengan pesan-pesan universal
Beautiful illuminated manuscript scroll opened showing Arabic calligraphy. Surrounding decorative elements include shaking hands, scales of justice, heart symbol. Traditional Islamic manuscript art style. Rich colors cream parchment, gold text, blue and red accents.

### slide-04 — Anak-anak berbagai latar belakang bergandengan tangan
Group of children from different backgrounds holding hands together. Various skin colors, different traditional clothing, happy faces. Silhouette of Ka'bah with soft light in background. Unity in diversity scene showing universal brotherhood and lasting values.
