## Base Palette
- Sandy gold, warm beige, dusty earth
- Dramatic sky contrasts (battle scenes)

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **Abrahah**: Yemeni king, ornate red/gold robe, short dark beard, golden crown, arrogant posture
- **Gajah Mahmud**: Largest war elephant, dark gray, red war saddle with gold cloth, much bigger than other elephants
- **Abdul Muthalib**: Elderly dignified Arab leader, white beard, clean white robe, calm noble posture
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.

## Slides

### slide-01 — Abrahah membangun gereja al-Qullays
Abrahah standing proudly in front of the grand al-Qullays church in Sanaa, Yemen. Massive pillars, gold ornaments, domed roof. Mountains of Sanaa in background. Mood: proud but hollow.

### slide-02 — Orang Arab tetap pergi ke Ka'bah
Split composition: LEFT side shows the grand al-Qullays church empty, no visitors. RIGHT side shows streams of Arab travelers on camels heading toward a distant simple stone cube in golden light. Contrast between ignored grandeur and beloved simplicity.

### slide-03 — Pasukan gajah bersiap berangkat
Military camp in Yemen. Abrahah on a raised platform giving orders. Rows of war elephants being prepared. Mahmud the biggest elephant stands front and center, towering over others. Soldiers lining up. Mood: menacing preparation.

### slide-04 — Pasukan berjalan melintasi padang pasir
Long marching column crossing desert LEFT TO RIGHT toward Makkah. Mahmud leads at front, trunk raised. Behind him: smaller elephants, cavalry, infantry with banners. Dust clouds rising. Hijaz hills in distance. Mood: overwhelming military power.

### slide-05 — Pasukan mengambil unta di dekat Makkah
Soldiers seizing 200 camels from a grazing field near Makkah. Camels being herded away by soldiers. In background, outskirts of Makkah visible. A few Arab herders watch helplessly. Mood: aggression, theft.

### slide-06 — Abdul Muthalib menemui Abrahah di tenda
Inside ornate military tent. Abdul Muthalib standing tall and calm faces Abrahah seated on throne, surrounded by guards, looking surprised. Between them: tension. Through tent opening: camels visible outside. Mood: dignity vs power.

### slide-07 — Ka'bah punya Tuhan yang akan melindunginya
Close-up moment: Abdul Muthalib hand raised in calm gesture of certainty. Abrahah leaning forward in disbelief. Light coming through tent opening illuminating Abdul Muthalib. Mood: unshakeable faith. Different angle from slide 06.

### slide-08 — Gajah Mahmud berlutut menolak maju
Mahmud the elephant KNEELING on the ground, front legs folded, head bowed LOW. He FACES TOWARD the stone cube visible in the distance ahead but REFUSES to move. Handler on top whipping desperately but elephant will not budge. Other elephants behind also stopping. Mood: divine intervention.

### slide-09 — Burung ababil datang dari langit
Sky FILLED with hundreds of small dark birds flying in formation from above. Each bird carries 3 small glowing reddish stones, 1 in beak, 1 in each claw. Stones raining down on panicking soldiers below. Dramatic sky -- dark storm on one side, golden light over the stone cube on other side. Mood: divine punishment, awe-inspiring.

### slide-10 — Kehancuran pasukan Abrahah
Aftermath scene: abandoned military equipment scattered on desert ground -- broken saddles, torn banners, empty armor. No dead bodies shown. Dust settling. In the distance, the stone cube stands untouched, bathed in golden light. A few birds still circling in sky. Mood: aftermath, protected.

### slide-11 — Malam kelahiran Nabi Muhammad
Night scene in Makkah. A simple stone house with WARM GOLDEN LIGHT emanating from within -- glowing through windows and door, spreading outward. Night sky filled with extraordinarily bright stars, one brilliant star directly above the house. Crescent moon. Silhouette of Makkah rooftops. A few women from behind walking toward the house. NO baby, NO human figure representing the Prophet. Only LIGHT from inside the house.
