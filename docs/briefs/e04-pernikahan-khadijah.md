## Base Palette
- Warm cream #fdf6e3, soft gold #d4a574, earthy brown #8b6914, muted green accents
- Consistent across ALL slides
- Lighting progression: Morning -- afternoon -- golden hour -- evening -- night

## Architecture
- Pre-Islamic Mecca: simple stone/mud-brick, flat roofs, date palms
- Always mention "6th century" and "mud-brick" for consistency

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **Khadijah**: Elegant businesswoman, hijab matching her clothing color + niqab -- face veil, only eyes visible, neat clean clothing in cream/white, dignified posture
- **Maysarah**: Middle-aged Arab man, simple traveler clothing, loyal appearance
- **Nafisah**: Modest Arab woman, warm approachable face
- **Abu Thalib**: Middle-aged Arab man, modest brown/beige robe, kind face
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.

## Slides

### slide-01 — Khadijah, wanita luar biasa di Makkah
Wide establishing. Meccan marketplace. Khadijah stands in front of her trading stall. Camels carry trade goods nearby. She speaks with an elderly merchant. Warm morning light. Mood: energy, pride.

### slide-02 — Khadijah dikenal sebagai al-Tahirah
Medium. Khadijah in courtyard of her home, handing a package to a grateful woman with children. A servant carries bundles behind her. Home well-kept but not ostentatious. Warm morning light. Mood: generosity, noble character.

### slide-03 — Maysarah melapor kepada Khadijah
Medium interior. Maysarah kneeling before Khadijah, hands animated in storytelling. Khadijah leans forward with shining eyes. Trade goods from Syria in background -- overflowing bundles. Afternoon light. Mood: amazement, growing admiration.

### slide-04 — Muhammad memimpin kafilah Khadijah ke Syam
Wide. Large trade caravan departing Makkah. Camels loaded with fine goods. Maysarah walks alongside. A GOLDEN GLOW leads the caravan at front. Desert road stretching forward. Sunrise light. Mood: departure, trust, new beginning. Several Arab traders and workers walking alongside.

### slide-05 — Khadijah mendengar tentang Muhammad al-Amin
Medium interior. Khadijah sits on cushions listening to an Arab man friend in simple robes gesturing toward the window. A FAINT GOLDEN GLOW visible through the window. Late morning light. Mood: fateful moment, interest awakened.

### slide-06 — Khadijah melamar Muhammad melalui Nafisah
Medium. Quiet garden/courtyard. Nafisah walking with purpose toward a GOLDEN GLOW in the distance ahead, carrying a message, gentle smile. Behind her, Khadijah watches from a window. Date palms and simple desert flowers. Golden hour light. Mood: love initiated, beautiful proposal.

### slide-07 — Abu Thalib datang melamar untuk Muhammad
Medium interior. Abu Thalib standing and speaking eloquently before seated elders. Elder Quraish men on carpets, nodding approvingly. A GOLDEN GLOW sits among them. Incense smoke rising. Warm lamplight, late afternoon interior. Mood: formal, honorable, happy occasion.

### slide-08 — Hari pernikahan
Wide interior. Simple Meccan home decorated for wedding. Small oil lamps glow in corners. Khadijah sits beside a GOLDEN GLOW. Family and guests gather with joyful faces. Festive meal on mats -- dates, bread, meat. Date palm in courtyard. Evening lamplight. Mood: happiness, celebration.

### slide-09 — 25 tahun bersama
Medium interior. A GOLDEN GLOW seated beside Khadijah in a peaceful home. Between them: scrolls, a small chest. Through window, Makkah skyline with simple stone cube visible. Khadijah has slight gray in her hair, warm content expression. Evening golden hour. Mood: deep love, faithful companionship.

### slide-10 — Cinta sejati bukan karena harta
Wide shot. Simple mud-brick home at golden hour. On one side, camels and trade goods sit untouched, fading into shadow. On other side, warm golden light fills a humble room where Khadijah sits peacefully beside a GOLDEN GLOW radiating warmth. Contrast: material wealth in shadow, love in light. Mood: true love transcends wealth.

### slide-11 — Muhammad selalu mengenang Khadijah
Close-up interior. A GOLDEN GLOW sits alone in a simple room, facing a window. On the mat: a folded shawl and small vase with desert flowers. Dusk, warm fading golden light through window. Mood: remembrance, eternal love, bittersweet. NO human figure -- only golden glow and belongings.

### slide-12 — Salam Allah untuk Khadijah
Wide establishing. Night sky above Makkah. From the heavens, soft divine golden-white light descends through clouds and envelops a simple house below. Simple stone cube silhouette in distance. Stars shine extraordinarily bright. A luminous white angelic form with no facial features descends carrying divine light. Night with moonlight and divine glow. Mood: ultimate honor, divine blessing.
