# E111 — Pertempuran Ajnadayn (12 slides)

## Base Palette
- Golden sand, distant green land, blue sky, hopeful expedition
- Yellow-orange harsh desert, blue scorching sky, determined endurance
- Brown dust, steel grey armor, green Islamic banners, heroic battle
- Green trees, warm brown, golden wisdom of ethical warfare
- Golden dawn, green banners, blue morning sky, hopeful victory
- Green Syam landscape, blue sky, golden sunset reflection

## Character Lock
- **Khalid ibn al-Walid**: A FIERCE WARRIOR with a THICK BLACK BEARD and sharp eyes. He wears CHAINMAIL ARMOR over DARK ROBES and a RED TURBAN. Muscular athletic build, battle-scarred. Commanding military stance
- **Amr ibn al-Ash**: A strategic Muslim commander about 40 with MEDIUM BLACK BEARD. He wears MILITARY ROBES in earth tones with lightweight armor. Intelligent eyes, tactical mind
- **Abu Bakr**: A slim, gentle-looking Arab man about 60 with a LIGHT BROWN/REDDISH BEARD now showing age. He wears WHITE KHALIFAH ROBES. Leadership posture, wise expression
- **Muslim Soldiers**: Warriors in earth-toned military clothing with round shields and straight swords. Green banners with no text
- **Roman Soldiers**: Heavy infantry in steel armor with large shields and spears. Disciplined formation
