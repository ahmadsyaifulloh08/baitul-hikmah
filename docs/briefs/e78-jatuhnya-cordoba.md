## Base Palette
- Cordoba gold and white Andalusi architecture
- Red Christian banners vs green Islamic banners
- River blues, siege grays, sunset oranges

## Character Lock
- **Fernando III**: Christian king in royal armor with red cape, golden crown, commanding presence
- **Ibn Hud**: Muslim ruler of Cordoba in fine robes, turban, dignified but worried expression
- **Muslim defenders**: Soldiers in simple armor over earth-toned robes, determined faces
- **Christian army**: Knights in metal armor with red crosses, large shields, imposing formation

## Slides

### slide-01 — Cordoba di masa kejayaan vs ancaman dari utara
Establishing shot split composition. LEFT: Beautiful Cordoba with Great Mosque golden dome, white Andalusi buildings, flowing river. RIGHT: Dark clouds and distant Christian army approaching. Red and white horseshoe arches visible in mosque. Colors: gold dome, white marble, blue river, red banners, dark storm clouds. Mood: beauty under threat.

### slide-02 — Pengepungan Cordoba oleh Fernando III
Wide shot of siege warfare around Cordoba walls. Trebuchet machines launching stones at city walls. Fernando III on horseback directing siege operations. Muslim defenders on ramparts. Siege camp with red cross banners surrounding city. Colors: gray stone walls, red crosses, brown siege machines, white tents. Mood: military pressure, defensive determination.

### slide-03 — Interior Masjid Agung dengan pilar merah-putih ikonik
Low angle interior shot of Cordoba Great Mosque famous red and white striped horseshoe arches extending in rows. Warm golden light filtering through. Empty prayer hall suggesting transition period. Arabic calligraphy on walls. Colors: red and white arches, gold calligraphy, white marble floors, warm amber lighting. Mood: architectural magnificence, quiet transformation.

### slide-04 — Kafilah hijrah Muslim Cordoba menuju Granada  
Wide shot of long refugee caravan leaving Cordoba toward Granada. Muslim families with camels, horses, carts carrying belongings and books. Cordoba small in background, rolling hills ahead. Sunset lighting. Colors: orange sunset sky, brown camels, white clothing, blue distant mountains, small golden Cordoba behind. Mood: sorrowful departure, hope for future.
