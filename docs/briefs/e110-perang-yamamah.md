## Character Lock
- **Khalid ibn al-Walid**: A FIERCE WARRIOR with a THICK BLACK BEARD and sharp eyes. He wears CHAINMAIL ARMOR over DARK ROBES and a RED TURBAN. Muscular athletic build, battle-scarred. Commanding military stance. Known as Sword of Allah
- **Al-Barra bin Malik**: A brave young Arab warrior about 25 with SHORT DARK BEARD. He wears SIMPLE BATTLE ARMOR and carries shield and spear. Heroic expression, fearless posture
- **Muslim Soldiers**: Various companions wearing SIMPLE ARMOR over EARTH-TONED ROBES. Round shields, straight swords, spears. Green or white BANNERS with no text. Determined faces
