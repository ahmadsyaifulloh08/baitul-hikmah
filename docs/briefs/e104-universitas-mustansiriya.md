- **Professor Ibn al-Salah**: An elderly hadith scholar about 60 with a LONG WHITE BEARD. He wears CREAM/WHITE SCHOLAR ROBES. Gentle eyes, traditional teaching presence
- **International Students**: Mixed ethnicities from across Islamic world, wearing traditional clothing of their regions
