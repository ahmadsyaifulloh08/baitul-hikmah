## Slides

### slide-01 — Pedagang Arab tiba di pelabuhan Aceh dan Sumatera
Wide shot of traditional sailing ships arriving at Aceh harbor with Arab and Persian merchants. Local Indonesian traders welcoming international visitors. Tropical Indonesian landscape with palm trees. Beginning of Islamic influence through trade. Colors: blue Indonesian seas, brown wooden ships, green tropical vegetation, white trader robes, gold spice trade goods. Mood: peaceful arrival, commercial welcome, cultural beginning.

### slide-02 — Kerajaan Samudera Pasai sebagai pusat Islam pertama
Medium shot of Samudera Pasai palace with first Indonesian Muslim ruler receiving Islamic scholars. Local architecture beginning to incorporate Islamic elements. Quran being taught alongside local customs. Cultural synthesis in early Islamic kingdom. Colors: brown Indonesian wooden architecture, green Islamic influences, gold royal decorations, white scholarly robes, blue peaceful atmosphere. Mood: political conversion, royal acceptance, cultural adaptation.

### slide-03 — Penyebaran Islam melalui pedagang dan pernikahan
Close-up of intermarriage ceremony between Arab merchant and Indonesian noble family. Cultural exchange through family connections. Children learning both Islamic and local traditions. Peaceful integration through personal relationships. Colors: red Indonesian ceremonial dress, white Islamic elements, green marriage celebration, gold family prosperity, brown tropical setting. Mood: personal integration, family bonds, cultural harmony.

### slide-04 — Jaringan perdagangan Islam di Nusantara
Establishing shot map view of Islamic trade network connecting Aceh, Palembang, Java, and Maluku with Middle Eastern and Indian ports. Ships carrying both goods and Islamic knowledge across Indonesian archipelago. Economic and spiritual connection. Colors: blue ocean trade routes, green Indonesian islands, brown trading vessels, gold spice wealth, white Islamic knowledge exchange. Mood: network expansion, economic prosperity, spiritual spread.
