## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.
- **Buraq**: Beautiful white winged creature, larger than donkey but smaller than horse, radiant white coat, graceful wings
- **Jibril**: Bright radiant light figure with wings, no specific human features
- **Other Prophets**: Silhouettes in white robes with gentle light around them
