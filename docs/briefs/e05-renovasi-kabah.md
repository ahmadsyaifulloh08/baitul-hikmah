## Ka'bah Architectural Notes (HISTORICAL ACCURACY)

### SEBELUM renovasi (slide 01, 03):
- Tinggi hanya ~9 hasta (~4 meter) -- setinggi orang dewasa berdiri + sedikit
- TANPA ATAP -- terbuka ke langit
- Dinding dari batu gunung yang DISUSUN TANPA PEREKAT (dry-stone masonry)
- Bentuk kubus sederhana, warna batu alam abu-abu/coklat gelap
- Hajar Aswad tertanam di sudut tenggara, setinggi ~1.5 meter dari tanah
- TIDAK ADA kiswah/kain penutup hitam (itu era jauh kemudian)
- TIDAK ADA minaret, dome, atau ornamen apapun
- Di sekitarnya: area terbuka (belum ada bangunan Masjidil Haram megah)
- Fondasi asli dari era Ibrahim AS masih terlihat di bagian bawah
- Hijr Ismail belum ada (terbentuk SETELAH renovasi karena dana kurang)

### SETELAH renovasi (slide 04, 08-10):
- Lebih TINGGI dari sebelumnya (~18 hasta, hampir 2x lipat)
- Kini punya ATAP dari kayu (kayu dari kapal Romawi yang karam)
- Dinding lebih rapi -- batu disusun dengan perekat
- Pintu masuk DIANGKAT tinggi dari tanah (sebelumnya setinggi tanah)
- Bagian Hijr Ismail terbentuk -- area setengah lingkaran di sisi utara yang TIDAK tercakup renovasi karena dana halal terbatas
- Tetap sederhana -- batu alam, TANPA ornamen mewah

### Environment Makkah 605 M:
- Lembah kering dikelilingi bukit-bukit berbatu (Jabal Abu Qubays, dll)
- Rumah-rumah sederhana dari batu dan mud-brick, atap datar
- Pasar perdagangan di sekitar Ka'bah
- TIDAK ADA bangunan bertingkat atau megah
- Pohon kurma jarang (Makkah tandus, bukan oasis)

### LARANGAN VISUAL:
- ❌ Ka'bah modern (besar, kiswah hitam, emas)
- ❌ Masjidil Haram modern (tiang, kubah, menara)
- ❌ Berhala/patung di dalam atau sekitar Ka'bah (jangan tampilkan)
- ❌ Ka'bah terlalu kecil atau tidak proporsional
- ✅ Tampilkan Ka'bah dengan HORMAT -- bangunan suci walau sederhana
- ✅ Pencahayaan warm/golden untuk kesan keagungan meski bentuk primitif

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. WAIST height of adults. ALWAYS this exact form.
- **Al-Walid bin al-Mughirah**: Elderly Arab chief, long grey beard, strong build, dark brown robes with a tan head cloth, authoritative posture
- **Abu Umayyah**: Very old Arab elder, white beard, lean frame, white robes, calm wise expression
- **Baqum**: Roman carpenter, lighter skin, short brown hair, simple work tunic, muscular arms, foreign appearance among Arabs

## Slides

### slide-01 — Ka'bah sebelum renovasi, rusak oleh banjir
Wide establishing. A LOW stone cube about 4 meters tall, dry-stone walls without mortar, NO ROOF open to the sky. Cracks visible on walls, flood water stains at base, some stones displaced. Simple ancient sacred building in a dry valley surrounded by rocky hills. Quraisy men in robes gathered around, pointing at damage with worried faces. Early morning warm light. Mood: concern, urgency, reverence for a damaged holy site.

### slide-02 — Kapal Romawi karam di pelabuhan Jeddah
Wide coastal scene. Wrecked Roman trading ship tilted on shallow water near rocky Jeddah shore. Arab men pulling timber and wooden planks from wreckage. Camels waiting on beach to carry materials. Baqum stands near the ship inspecting wood. Bright midday sun. Mood: opportunity from misfortune.

### slide-03 — Al-Walid berani memulai pembongkaran
Medium shot. Al-Walid bin al-Mughirah holding a tool, carefully removing the first stones from the LOW dry-stone wall of the roofless Ka'bah. The wall is only slightly taller than a man. Crowd of Quraisy men watching from distance, some with hands raised in prayer, some stepping back in awe. Dry valley and rocky hills behind. Desert sun. Mood: bravery, tension, sacred hesitation.

### slide-04 — Gotong royong membangun Ka'bah baru
Wide shot. Multiple groups of Arab men from different clans building new Ka'bah walls with stone and mortar. Walls now rising TALLER than the old structure. Baqum the Roman carpenter directing wooden roof beam installation at the top. Timber planks from a ship visible nearby. Camels carrying stones. The new doorway placed HIGH above ground level. Rocky hills of Makkah valley behind. Warm afternoon light. Mood: unity, collective effort, sacred progress.

### slide-05 — Sengketa siapa yang meletakkan Hajar Aswad
Medium courtyard scene. Four clan leaders standing face to face near the nearly-complete Ka'bah, angry gestures, heated argument. One group shows blood-stained hands from a blood oath pact. Other Quraisy men watching anxiously from sides. The Ka'bah behind them has a visible gap at the southeast corner where the dark Hajar Aswad stone should be placed. Late afternoon, tense dramatic shadows. Mood: conflict, tribal pride, near-violence.

### slide-06 — Abu Umayyah mengusulkan solusi
Medium shot. Abu Umayyah standing among seated angry clan leaders, one hand raised for calm, speaking with authority. Men around him listening reluctantly. Entrance gate of Masjidil Haram visible behind him. Golden hour light. Mood: wisdom breaking through conflict.

### slide-07 — Muhammad al-Amin memasuki Masjidil Haram
Wide shot from inside looking toward entrance. A GOLDEN GLOW enters through the main gate of Masjidil Haram. Quraisy men on both sides turn toward the light with relieved expressions, some smiling. Partially rebuilt Ka'bah walls visible. Warm golden hour light streaming through entrance. Mood: hope, relief, trust.

### slide-08 — Solusi brilian: kain dan Hajar Aswad
Wide overhead angle. A GOLDEN GLOW stands at center. Four clan representatives each holding one corner of a large white cloth. On the cloth sits the dark Hajar Aswad stone. Men lifting the cloth together toward Ka'bah wall. Crowd watching with amazement. Sunset golden light. Mood: genius solution, unity restored.

### slide-09 — Muhammad meletakkan Hajar Aswad
Close-medium. Golden light emanating from where a GOLDEN GLOW reaches toward the Ka'bah corner wall, placing the dark rounded Hajar Aswad stone into its position. Clan leaders stand nearby, smiling with satisfaction. Warm sunset glow illuminating the scene. Mood: honor, peace, completion.

### slide-10 — Ka'bah berdiri megah kembali, simbol persatuan
Wide establishing. The renovated Ka'bah stands TALLER than before with neat stone walls, new wooden roof beams, and raised doorway. Simple but dignified sacred cube in the valley. Quraisy families gathered peacefully around it in the open courtyard. A GOLDEN GLOW stands among the crowd, radiating warm light. Rocky hills of Makkah and simple mud-brick houses frame the valley. Dusk sky with warm orange-purple gradient. Mood: unity, hope, a sacred house renewed.
