# E08 — Dakwah Terang (10 slides)

## Base Palette
- Golden morning light, brown hills, blue sky
- Warm interior yellows, dramatic lighting contrasts
- Red anger vs white-gold peace

## Character Lock
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward
- **Abu Lahab**: A heavy-set, wealthy Arab man about 50 with a THICK BLACK BEARD. He wears RICH DARK RED/MAROON ROBES with gold trim. Arrogant expression, imposing stance. Red angry face when shouting
