## Slides

### slide-01 — Persiapan pertempuran di Ain Jalut Palestine
Establishing shot of two armies preparing for battle at Ain Jalut valley. Mamluk forces under green banners positioned strategically. Mongol army across valley in traditional steppe formation. Tense pre-battle atmosphere in Palestinian countryside. Colors: green Mamluk banners, brown battlefield terrain, blue Palestinian sky, gold Mamluk armor, dark Mongol leather equipment. Mood: strategic confrontation, historical moment.

### slide-02 — Qutuz dan Baibars memimpin serangan Mamluk
Medium shot of Mamluk cavalry charge led by Sultan Qutuz and General Baibars. Elite Mamluk horsemen in disciplined formation attacking Mongol lines. Tactical superiority of trained cavalry against nomadic raiders. Decisive military leadership. Colors: green Mamluk cavalry charge, brown horses in motion, gold flashing weapons, blue battle sky, white horse foam. Mood: military excellence, tactical genius, turning tide.

### slide-03 — Kekalahan pasukan Mongol pertama kali
Wide shot showing Mongol retreat after first major defeat. Previously invincible Mongol army fleeing battlefield. Mamluk victory breaking myth of Mongol invincibility. Historical turning point for Islamic world. Scattered Mongol equipment on field. Colors: brown retreating Mongol cavalry, green victorious Mamluk banners, gray scattered battlefield, blue sky of victory, red sunset of change. Mood: historic victory, invincibility broken.

### slide-04 — Dampak kemenangan menyelamatkan dunia Islam
Close-up of Damascus and Cairo celebrating news of Ain Jalut victory. Muslim population rejoicing at salvation from Mongol threat. Islamic civilization preserved to continue in Egypt and Syria. Messenger bringing victory news to celebrating crowds. Colors: gold celebration in cities, green Islamic relief, blue joyful skies, white victory banners, brown messenger horses. Mood: salvation celebration, civilization preserved, hope restored.
