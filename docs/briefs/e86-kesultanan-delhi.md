## Slides

### slide-02 — Pembangunan Qutb Minar dan arsitektur Indo-Islamic
Establishing shot of workers constructing towering Qutb Minar minaret using Indian craftsmanship with Islamic design. Hindu artisans adapting their skills to Islamic geometric patterns. Fusion of architectural traditions creating new Indo-Islamic style. Colors: red sandstone construction, gold Islamic calligraphy, brown wooden scaffolding, green geometric patterns, blue construction sky. Mood: cultural synthesis, architectural innovation, skilled craftsmanship.

### slide-03 — Penyebaran Islam melalui para Sufi di India
Medium shot of Sufi saints teaching in peaceful garden setting to mixed audience of Indians from different backgrounds. Chishti and other Sufi orders spreading Islam through love and wisdom rather than force. Diverse followers learning spiritual teachings. Colors: green garden setting, white Sufi robes, brown Indian earth, blue peaceful water, gold spiritual illumination. Mood: peaceful conversion, spiritual attraction, inclusive teaching.

### slide-04 — Kemakmuran perdagangan dan kebudayaan campuran
Close-up of bustling Delhi market with merchants selling goods from across Asia. Indian spices, Central Asian textiles, Persian books. Hindu and Muslim traders working together. Cultural exchange and economic prosperity under Islamic rule. Colors: orange Indian spices, blue Central Asian textiles, gold Persian manuscripts, green market abundance, brown trade goods. Mood: economic prosperity, cultural exchange, commercial harmony.
