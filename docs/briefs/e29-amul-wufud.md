## Base Palette
- Ancient map browns, golden light, earth tones
- Multicultural clothing colors in harmony

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.
- **Arab Merchants**: Arab merchants in COLORFUL ROBES mix of brown, white, blue, red. Various beard styles. Head cloths keffiyeh in white or checked patterns. Weathered faces from desert travel.
