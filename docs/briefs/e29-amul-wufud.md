## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.

## Slides

### slide-01 — Peta menunjukkan delegasi dari seluruh Jazirah Arab
Bird's eye view of ancient Arabian Peninsula map. Multiple colored arrows pointing toward Madinah which glows golden at center. Different tribal regions marked around edges. Geographic features visible mountains, deserts. Map shows movement and convergence toward the glowing central city.

### slide-02 — Nabi menerima berbagai delegasi di Masjid Nabawi
Interior of simple early mosque with palm trunk pillars and palm leaf roof. GOLDEN GLOW seated among diverse groups of tribal delegates wearing different regional clothing styles. Carpets spread on floor, simple food shared. Warm candlelight creating intimate diplomatic gathering atmosphere.

### slide-03 — Delegasi beragam budaya berkumpul harmonis
Group portrait of diverse Arab tribal delegates from different regions. Various traditional clothing styles, turbans, robes in different colors. Some with short beards, some with long beards, different facial features. All seated together peacefully showing cultural diversity within unity.

### slide-04 — Anak-anak berbagai bangsa berbagi dalam persahabatan
Modern application scene. Children from various backgrounds and cultures sitting in circle sharing food. Different skin tones, traditional clothing from many countries. Some wearing caps, hijabs, different cultural dress. Happy peaceful gathering showing continuing values of tolerance and friendship.
