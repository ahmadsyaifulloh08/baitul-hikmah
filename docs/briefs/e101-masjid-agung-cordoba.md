## Base Palette
- Brown parchment, blue sea, green land
- Red brick, white limestone, brown wood, zebra pattern
- Golden manuscripts, blue mosaics, warm library lighting
- Warm night lights, golden mosque lighting, starry blue night

## Character Lock
- **Abd al-Rahman I**: A determined Arab prince about 30 with a SHORT DARK BEARD. He wears TRAVEL-WORN ROBES in earthy colors, carrying royal insignia. Survivor expression, strong leadership posture
- **International Architects**: Mixed workers from various backgrounds - Arab, Mozarab, Visigoth craftsmen with tools
- **Scholars and Craftsmen**: Various ages and ethnicities working on manuscripts and mosaics
