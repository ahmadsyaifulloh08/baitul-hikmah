## Character Lock
- **Bilal ibn Rabbah**: A young man about 30 with DARK BROWN SKIN Ethiopian. Strong build despite hardship. He wears SIMPLE WHITE/OFF-WHITE CLOTHING initially torn/worn as slave, later clean. Bright determined eyes, dignified posture even in suffering
- **Umayyah ibn Khalaf**: A heavy-set, wealthy Arab man about 50 with a THICK BLACK BEARD. He wears RICH DARK RED/MAROON ROBES with gold trim. Arrogant expression, imposing stance. Represents cruelty and power
- **Abu Bakr al-Siddiq**: A slim, gentle-looking Arab man about 38 with a LIGHT BROWN/REDDISH BEARD, medium length. He wears WHITE ROBES and a WHITE TURBAN. Soft features, kind eyes, compassionate expression
