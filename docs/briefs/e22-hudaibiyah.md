## Base Palette
- White ihram, golden sand, blue sky hopeful journey
- Brown rock, white clothing, miraculous wonder
- Green shade, white clothing, brown earth solemn courage
- White and dark red, tense but hopeful negotiation

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.
- **Uthman ibn Affan**: Dignified gentle Arab man with medium light brown beard. He wears fine white robes. Soft features, kind eyes, elegant bearing. Known for generosity and modesty
- **Umar ibn al-Khattab**: Tall powerfully built Arab man with long dark beard. He wears dark green robes and black turban. Strong jawline, intense piercing eyes, commanding presence
- **1400 companions in ihram**: Large group of pilgrims in simple white clothing
