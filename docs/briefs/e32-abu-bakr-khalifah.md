## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **Abu Bakr**: A slim, gentle-looking Arab man about 38 years old with a LIGHT BROWN/REDDISH BEARD, medium length. He wears WHITE ROBES and a WHITE TURBAN. Soft features, kind eyes, compassionate expression. Wealthy but humble appearance.
- **Umar**: A TALL, POWERFULLY BUILT Arab man with a LONG DARK BEARD black with grey in later events. He wears DARK GREEN ROBES and a BLACK TURBAN. Strong jawline, intense piercing eyes, commanding presence. Known for his stern but just demeanor.
