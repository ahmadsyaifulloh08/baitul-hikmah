## Base Palette
- Spanish court golds and reds
- Inquisition black and gray shadows
- Hope blues and escape whites

## Character Lock
- **Torquemada**: Grand Inquisitor in black robes, stern authoritative face, religious authority
- **Spanish nobles**: Court officials in rich dress, participating in persecutions
- **Conversos**: Former Muslims/Jews in Spanish dress but maintaining secret faith
- **Refugee families**: Mixed religious groups fleeing persecution together

## Slides

### slide-01 — Pembentukan Inkuisisi Spanyol 1478
Medium shot of Spanish royal court with Ferdinand and Isabella issuing papal decree. Torquemada in black Dominican robes receiving authority. Court officials witnessing formal establishment of religious tribunal. Serious governmental atmosphere. Colors: red royal banners, black inquisitor robes, gold papal documents, white formal declarations. Mood: official persecution begins, institutional power.

### slide-02 — Penyembunyian identitas dan praktik agama rahasia
Close-up of converso family secretly practicing Islamic prayers in hidden basement room. Quran concealed inside Christian books. Children learning dual identity. Tense atmosphere of concealment and faith preservation. Colors: brown hidden cellar, gold secret Quranic pages, white prayer clothing, green spiritual perseverance, amber candlelight. Mood: dangerous faith, underground survival.

### slide-03 — Auto de fe dan pengusiran massal 1492
Wide shot of public ceremony with conversos forced to flee Spain carrying belongings. Ships in harbor loading refugees. Mixed crowds of Muslims, Jews, and sympathetic Christians departing together. Solemn exodus scene. Colors: gray ships, brown bundled possessions, blue Mediterranean sea, white farewell clothing, orange sunset departure. Mood: sorrowful departure, religious freedom lost.

### slide-04 — Diaspora dan warisan budaya di negara baru
Establishing shot of former Spanish Muslims and Jews establishing new communities in Ottoman Empire, North Africa, and Americas. Sephardic synagogues and Islamic schools in new lands. Cultural preservation across continents. Colors: green Ottoman minarets, blue ocean crossings, gold new architectural styles, white fresh start communities, brown preserved manuscripts. Mood: resilience, cultural survival, new beginnings.
