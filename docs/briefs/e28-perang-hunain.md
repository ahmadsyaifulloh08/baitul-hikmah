## Base Palette
- Desert browns, orange sunset, grey stone
- Dawn greys transitioning to golden light

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.
- **Malik bin Auf**: Young brave Arab commander about 30 years old with SHORT DARK BEARD. Athletic build, fierce determined expression. Wears DESERT ROBES with warrior accessories. Commanding stance.
