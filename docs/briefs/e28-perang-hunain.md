## Base Palette
- Desert browns, orange sunset, grey stone
- Dawn greys transitioning to golden light

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.
- **Malik bin Auf**: Young brave Arab commander about 30 years old with SHORT DARK BEARD. Athletic build, fierce determined expression. Wears DESERT ROBES with warrior accessories. Commanding stance.

## Slides

### slide-01 — Malik bin Auf memimpin persiapan perang
Medium shot of Malik bin Auf standing proudly before his gathered army. Behind him, long caravan of camels carrying families and treasures. Desert landscape with wind blowing sand. Warriors on horses, women and children visible in background. Ambitious preparation scene.

### slide-02 — Serangan mendadak di Lembah Hunain saat fajar
Wide shot of narrow rocky valley at dawn. Arrows flying from hidden positions behind rocks and hills. Muslim army caught by surprise in valley bottom. Dark silhouettes against grey dawn sky. One golden glowing figure stands firm while others scatter. Chaos and confusion.

### slide-03 — Pasukan muslimin bangkit kembali setelah dipanggil
Medium shot showing Muslim warriors regrouping and charging forward with renewed spirit. Banners raised high, determined expressions. GOLDEN GLOW moves forward leading the charge. Valley background with rocky outcrops. Moment of comeback and victory.

### slide-04 — Pengepungan kota Thaif yang bertembok tinggi
Low angle shot of massive stone walls of Thaif city. GOLDEN GLOW and Muslim army gathered peacefully outside the walls, hands raised in prayer. Evening sky with soft clouds. Walls too high to breach. Patient siege scene with spiritual atmosphere.
