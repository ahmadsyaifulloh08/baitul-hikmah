## Slides

### slide-01 — Al-Andalus masa kejayaan dengan penyusutan wilayah
Wide establishing shot showing glorious Al-Andalus with Cordoba prosperity in foreground, map overlay showing Islamic territory shrinking northward. Muslim, Christian, Jewish scholars working together in markets and libraries. Granada isolated as last Islamic kingdom. Colors: gold Cordoba prosperity, blue interfaith harmony, red territorial loss, white architectural magnificence. Mood: nostalgia for golden age, mounting pressure.

### slide-02 — Boabdil menyerahkan kunci Granada
Medium shot of ceremonial surrender at Granada gates. Sultan Boabdil in ornate Islamic robes handing golden city keys to Ferdinand and Isabella in royal Spanish dress. Alhambra palace visible in background. Formal but sorrowful moment. Colors: gold ceremonial keys, red Spanish royal banners, white Alhambra architecture, blue winter sky. Mood: dignified defeat, end of an era.

### slide-03 — Kontras pembakaran buku vs Alhambra yang bertahan  
Split composition showing LEFT: books burning in town square, destruction of Islamic knowledge. RIGHT: Alhambra palace standing eternal with intricate Arabic calligraphy and geometric patterns preserved. Contrast between destruction and survival. Colors: red flames vs gold Alhambra, black smoke vs white marble, brown burning books vs green surviving gardens. Mood: tragedy balanced by enduring legacy.

### slide-04 — Anak-anak dunia bersatu di depan Alhambra modern
Wide shot of children from different countries and religions holding hands in circle in front of illuminated Alhambra palace. World flags fluttering in background. Modern tourism and cultural appreciation. Rainbow diversity in clothing. Colors: gold illuminated Alhambra, rainbow flag diversity, green international friendship, white peaceful unity, blue evening sky. Mood: universal harmony, cultural bridge across centuries.
