## Character Lock
- **Rumi**: Young man with short dark beard, scholar robes, intelligent gentle eyes
- **Rumi's father**: Elderly scholar with long white beard, white turban, wise dignified appearance
- **Shams-i Tabrizi**: Middle-aged mystic with piercing eyes, simple dark robes, mysterious aura
- **Whirling Dervishes**: White flowing robes with tall felt caps, arms extended

## Slides

### slide-01 — Perjalanan keluarga dari Balkh ke Konya
Wide establishing shot. Caravan of Afghan refugees traveling through mountain pass from Balkh to Konya. Young Rumi holding his father's hand walking beside camels loaded with books and belongings. Afghan mountains in background. Desert travel colors: brown camels, blue mountains, white scholar turbans, green fabric bundles. Mood: spiritual journey, family protection.

### slide-02 — Rumi bertemu Shams di taman Konya
Medium shot in palace garden courtyard. Rumi listening intently to Shams-i Tabrizi under flowering trees near fountain. Both seated on Persian carpet. Books scattered around them. Garden colors: green trees, blue fountain water, white scholar robes, gold sunlight filtering through leaves. Mood: spiritual awakening, transformation moment.

### slide-03 — Para darwis Mevlevi melakukan tarian berputar
Close-up of whirling ceremony inside tekke hall. Multiple dervishes in white flowing robes spinning with arms extended, tall felt caps on heads. Rumi conducting in center. Arabic calligraphy on wooden walls. Interior colors: white robes, brown wood, gold Arabic script, warm lamplight. Mood: ecstatic devotion, divine union.

### slide-04 — Rumi menulis Masnavi di perpustakaan pribadinya  
Detail shot of Rumi writing by candlelight surrounded by Persian and Arabic manuscripts. Quill pen in hand writing on parchment. Shelves of ancient books, rolled scrolls, inkwell. Through window: bird in twilight sky. Study colors: gold candlelight, brown aged paper, green book covers, blue ink. Mood: contemplative inspiration, scholarly devotion.
