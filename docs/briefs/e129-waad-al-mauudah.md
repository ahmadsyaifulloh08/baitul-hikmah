## Base Palette
- Muted desert browns and greys for Jahiliyyah scenes
- Warm golden sunrise for Islam's arrival
- Soft pink and green for hope and new life
- Deep blue night sky with stars for spiritual scenes

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst. CHEST height of adults.
- **Arab father Jahiliyyah**: A middle-aged Arab man with dark beard, wearing dull brown robes, sorrowful downcast expression
- **Arab mother**: A woman in simple cream hijab and robes, protective posture, warm eyes full of love
- **Baby girl**: Wrapped in soft white cloth, peaceful innocent face, small and precious
- **Generic Arab men**: Men in earth-toned robes with various beards, some compassionate some indifferent

## ⚠️ SENSITIVITY RULES:
- **NO images of violence against children** — absolutely forbidden
- **NO burial scenes** — do not depict digging, sand covering, or any harm to babies
- Focus on: emotions, divine condemnation, rescue, Islamic reform, celebration of daughters
- Use SYMBOLS: empty cradle, protective hands, sunrise, blooming flowers
