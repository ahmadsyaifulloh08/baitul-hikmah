## Base Palette
- Desert journey: sandy gold, warm beige, clear blue sky
- Monastery: stone gray, warm interior browns
- Market: warm vibrant colors

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **Abu Thalib**: Middle-aged Arab man, modest brown/beige robe, kind face, not wealthy appearance
- **Bahira**: Elderly Christian monk, long white beard, dark brown/black monastic robes, wise gentle eyes, Busra monastery setting
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.

## Slides

### slide-01 — Muhammad dekat dengan pamannya
A warm courtyard in a modest Makkah house. Abu Thalib sitting on a mat organizing travel provisions -- leather bags, dried dates, water skins. A GOLDEN RADIANT GLOW sits close beside him. Other children play in background. Mood: closeness, family bond.

### slide-02 — Abu Thalib bersiap perjalanan dagang
Abu Thalib standing in front of a loaded camel, checking ropes and bundles. Desert road stretching into the distance. Other merchants preparing camels nearby. Morning pre-dawn light, cool blue-orange sky. Trade goods visible: fabric bundles, leather bags. Mood: preparation for a great journey.

### slide-03 — Muhammad ikut dalam perjalanan
A long camel caravan in the desert at sunrise. Abu Thalib rides at the front. A GOLDEN GLOW emanates from the camel beside him. Behind them, a line of other camels carry trade goods. Colors of golden sunrise, brown sand, clear blue sky. Wide panoramic composition -- caravan moving left to right. Mood: adventure and excitement.

### slide-04 — Kafilah tiba di Busra
Panoramic view of the town of Busra appearing on the horizon. The caravan approaching from the desert -- camels in a line, tired but relieved. Green trees and stone buildings of Busra contrast with sandy desert behind. A small monastery visible on the edge of town. Late afternoon golden light. Mood: arrival, relief.

### slide-05 — Bahira keluar mengundang kafilah
Bahira standing in the doorway of his simple stone monastery, gesturing warmly for the caravan to come inside. Arab merchants looking surprised. A long table with food visible through the open door. Mood: unexpected hospitality, mystery.

### slide-06 — Bahira bertanya apakah semua sudah hadir
Interior of Bahira monastery. Long wooden table with a simple meal -- bread, olives, figs, water. Arab merchants seated around it. Bahira standing at head of table, scanning every face carefully with sharp searching eyes. One EMPTY SEAT visible. Through the window: a camel with faint golden glow outside. Mood: suspense, investigation.

### slide-07 — Bahira menemukan tanda istimewa
Bahira kneeling with his hands raised in awe, facing a GOLDEN RADIANT GLOW. Abu Thalib standing behind with expression mixing surprise and concern. Soft golden light fills the room. Ancient scrolls/books visible on shelves. Stone monastery interior, warm tones. Mood: wonder, revelation, sacred.

### slide-08 — Bahira memperingatkan Abu Thalib
Close conversation scene: Bahira gripping Abu Thalib arm urgently, leaning in close, speaking with intensity. Abu Thalib expression: alarmed, protective. Behind them through doorway: the caravan camels and faint golden glow. Bahira pointing toward Makkah. Dark interior, dramatic side-lighting. Mood: urgent warning, danger.

### slide-09 — Pelajaran: Allah selalu melindungi
Symbolic scene: Simple stone cube at center with a protective dome of golden light arching over it. Desert landscape around it. Abu Thalib silhouette walking with a small golden light beside him, returning home. Sky shows crescent moon and protective stars forming a gentle arc. Mood: divine protection, care.

### slide-10 — Pelajaran: kasih sayang keluarga
Abu Thalib walking on a desert road back toward Makkah visible in distance. He walks protectively close to a GOLDEN GLOW beside him. Behind them, the town of Busra fades into distance. Late afternoon light, long shadows. His posture: protective, watchful. Mood: familial love, guardianship.

### slide-11 — Muhammad dikenal sebagai al-Amin
A lively Meccan market scene. People smiling and waving warmly. Bright market colors -- red, yellow, orange fabrics from merchant stalls. A GOLDEN WARM GLOW moves through the market center, and people around it look happy and trusting. Abu Thalib walks nearby, looking proud. Warm afternoon light. Mood: joyful homecoming, community trust.
