## Base Palette
- Warm earth tones, melancholic sunset oranges, gentle morning golds
- Desert landscape dominant

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **Halimah**: Bedouin woman, simple brown/cream clothing, head covering, kind gentle appearance
- **Ummu Aiman**: Dark-skinned woman, Ethiopian, simple modest clothing, protective/caring demeanor
- **Abu Thalib**: Middle-aged Arab man, modest brown/beige robe, kind face, not wealthy appearance
- **Abdul Muthalib**: Elderly dignified Arab leader, white beard, clean white robe, calm noble posture
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.

## Slides

### slide-01 — Abdullah wafat di Yatsrib
A simple fresh grave mound with plain stone marker in an oasis of Yatsrib. Date palms casting long shadows, late afternoon. A few travelers belongings -- merchant bags, water flask -- left near the grave. Golden-orange sunset sky, melancholic. Buildings of ancient Yatsrib in background. Mood: sad but peaceful.

### slide-02 — Harta peninggalan Abdullah
Still-life scene: five camels, a few goats, and a small bundle of belongings arranged in a simple courtyard in Makkah. Morning light. Empty -- the owner is gone. A woman shadow falls across the ground near the animals. Simple, sparse. Mood: modest inheritance, absence.

### slide-03 — Ibu-ibu susu datang ke Makkah
Market/gathering scene in Makkah. Several Bedouin women arriving on donkeys/camels, looking at babies held by Meccan mothers. Busy, colorful scene. Women examining, negotiating. Focus on social activity. Mood: lively tradition.

### slide-04 — Halimah tidak mendapat bayi
Halimah sitting alone on the edge of the gathering, looking dejected. Other Bedouin women walking away with babies. Her thin donkey and skinny goat nearby. Dry, barren look. Late afternoon. Mood: disappointment, poverty.

### slide-05 — Halimah mengambil bayi yatim
Halimah standing near a Meccan house, being handed a bundle of white cloth. A WARM GOLDEN GLOW emanates from the bundle. Halimah expression: surprised, touched. Mood: turning point, quiet miracle. NO baby face/body visible. Just cloth bundle with golden light.

### slide-06 — Keberkahan di Bani Sad
Halimah camp in the desert, but everything is LUSH and GREEN. Fat healthy goats grazing on green grass. Full milk containers. Halimah tent looks better maintained. A warm golden glow emanates from near the tent. Other Bani Sad tents in background look dry/normal by comparison. Mood: miraculous blessing.

### slide-07 — Kolom cahaya di padang Bani Sad
A brilliant COLUMN OF WHITE-GOLDEN LIGHT rising from the ground up to the sky in an open field. NO human figure inside or near the light -- pure nur. Two luminous silhouettes, angels with no facial features, just glowing white forms, kneeling on either side. Sheep/goats in distance looking toward the light. Children running away toward tents. Mood: supernatural, sacred.

### slide-08 — Halimah khawatir, membawa kembali ke Makkah
Halimah on a donkey traveling along a desert road toward Makkah visible ahead. She carries a bundle with golden glow. Her expression: worried, protective. Desert road stretching behind her. Mood: concern, maternal protection.

### slide-09 — Aminah mengajak ke Madinah
A small caravan, 2-3 camels, setting out from Makkah. A young woman, modest dress, rides a camel with a covered howdah. A small golden light sits beside her. Ummu Aiman walking alongside. Road ahead stretches toward distant hills. Morning light, hopeful departure. Mood: mother-son journey, bittersweet.

### slide-10 — Aminah wafat di al-Abwa
Desert roadside between Makkah and Madinah. A camel howdah stopped. Ummu Aiman from behind kneeling beside the sedan, head bowed in grief. A SMALL GOLDEN LIGHT stands alone in the vast empty desert -- dim but not extinguished. A pair of small sandals on the ground near the light. Purple/orange melancholic sunset sky. Mood: deep sadness, loneliness.

### slide-11 — Ummu Aiman membawa kembali ke Makkah
Ummu Aiman walking on foot along a desert road toward Makkah visible ahead. She leads a camel. A warm golden glow travels beside her. Stars beginning to appear in dusky sky. Her shadow stretches long. She looks determined despite exhaustion. Mood: devotion, protection, resilience.

### slide-12 — Abdul Muthalib dan cahaya Muhammad di Ka'bah
Abdul Muthalib sitting on a carpet/mat near the stone cube courtyard. Other Quraish leaders sitting in a circle, some smiling warmly. A WARM GOLDEN LIGHT sits close beside Abdul Muthalib -- he has one hand gently extended toward it. Afternoon light. Mood: love, warmth, grandfather devotion.

### slide-13 — Wafatnya Abdul Muthalib
An elderly man hand reaching out from a bed/mat, pointing toward a younger man, Abu Thalib, who stands nearby with hand on heart accepting responsibility. A small golden light between them -- being entrusted. Simple Meccan room, oil lamp, muted colors. Mood: passing of guardianship, solemn trust.

### slide-14 — Abu Thalib merawat dengan kasih sayang
Simple modest home interior. Abu Thalib sitting with his own children at a modest meal -- bread, dates, milk. A golden warm light among them. The light seems to make the modest meal glow warmer. Abu Thalib smiling toward the light. Cozy family scene. Mood: love despite poverty, warmth.

### slide-15 — Menggembala kambing di perbukitan
Hilltop overlooking Makkah valley. A shepherd staff planted in the ground with GOLDEN LIGHT emanating around it. A flock of 15-20 sheep/goats grazing peacefully below. One baby goat standing close to the staff/light. Golden morning hour light from behind. Makkah city small in the distance below. Mood: peaceful, humble, independent. NO human figure. Shepherd staff + golden light only.
