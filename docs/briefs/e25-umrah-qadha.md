## Base Palette
- White ihram with golden journey hope
- Black Kaaba with white ihram, bright blue sacred energy
- Golden sacred light, black Kaaba, white Bilal triumphant
- Joyful celebration of fulfilled promise

## Character Lock (copy-paste ke setiap prompt yang relevan)
- **GOLDEN GLOW**: A small warm circular golden-white light, about 15% of image height, with 5-7 soft rays radiating outward. ALWAYS this exact form -- no column, ribbon, burst, or starburst.
- **Bilal ibn Rabah**: Dark-skinned Abyssinian man with strong build and deep soulful eyes. He wears simple white robes. Former slave who became the first muezzin of Islam
- **2000 Muslim pilgrims**: Large group in white ihram clothing showing fulfillment of promise
