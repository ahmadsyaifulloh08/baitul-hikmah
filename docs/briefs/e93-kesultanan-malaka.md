## Slides

### slide-01 — Parameswara mendirikan Malaka dan konversi ke Islam

Foundation of major Southeast Asian Islamic sultanate. Strategic location chosen. Colors: brown Malay palace wood, green Islamic conversion, gold royal ceremony, blue Malacca Strait, white Islamic scholars. Mood: royal conversion, political transformation, religious adoption.

Colors: blue busy harbor, brown various trading ships, gold valuable cargo, green spice markets, white international cooperation. Mood: commercial prosperity, international hub, cultural diversity.

Network of Islamic sultanates developing. Regional Islamic expansion. Colors: green Islamic spread, blue maritime routes, brown traditional ships, gold new Islamic courts, white scholarly exchange. Mood: religious expansion, cultural influence, network building.

### slide-04 — Ancaman Portugis dan akhir zaman keemasan

End of golden age approaching with colonial arrival. Traditional vs modern military technology. Colors: red Portuguese threat, brown defensive preparations, blue uncertain waters, green Islamic resistance, white changing times. Mood: approaching crisis, colonial pressure, defensive preparation.
