## Slides

### slide-01 — Ibn Taimiyah muda belajar di perpustakaan Damascus

### slide-02 — Ibn Taimiyah mengajar di masjid Damascus
Medium shot of Ibn Taimiyah standing at wooden pulpit teaching large crowd of students in mosque courtyard. He gestures with authority wearing green scholar robes and white turban. ### slide-03 — Ibn Taimiyah menulis di penjara Qal'at Damascus

He continues scholarly work despite imprisonment. Colors: gray stone walls, brown wooden table, white manuscript pages, orange window light, black iron bars. Mood: perseverance, unbroken scholarly spirit.

### slide-04 — Pemakaman Ibn Taimiyah dihadiri ribuan orang
Wide shot of massive funeral procession through Damascus streets. Thousands of mourners from all social classes walking behind simple wooden coffin. Damascus city architecture in background. Despite controversy, people honor the great scholar. Colors: brown wooden coffin, white funeral shrouds, cream Damascus buildings, blue mourning crowd, golden respectful sunlight. Mood: honored farewell, scholarly legacy respected.
